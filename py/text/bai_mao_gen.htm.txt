(ou mao gen 歐 茅 根 )Pron. in Japanese:日語發音 ﹕bokonPron. in Korean:韓語發音 ﹕mogun, pakmogunPron. in Cantonese:粵語發音 ﹕maau4 gan1Other names:別名：bai mao gen 白茅根Common Name:英文名 ﹕imperata, rhizome of wooly grassDistribution:分佈 ﹕throughout china and Southeast Asia全 中 國 與 東 南 亞 。Properties (characteristics)﹕性味 ﹕sweet, cold甘 ﹐ 寒 。Channels (meridians) entered:歸經 ﹕lung, stomach, small intestine, bladder肺 ﹐ 胃 ﹐ 小 腸 ﹐ 膀 胱 。Actions & Indications:主治 ﹕cools the blood and stops bleeding; quells heat and promotes urination, clears heat from stomach and lungs涼 血 ﹐ 止 血 ﹐ 退 燒  ﹐ 利 尿 ﹐ 清 肺 熱 ﹐ 清 胃 熱 。Medical Function:藥理 ﹕(1). stops bleeding (2). anti bacteria and anti virus. (3). promotes urination, especially during 5~10 days while taking the herb. It can be due to the potassium salt in the herb.Chemical ingredients:化學成份 ﹕glucose 6.8%; cane sugar, fructose, and starch 21%, potassium salt, oxalic acid, carotene, vitamins, and anemonin.Dosage:用量 ﹕9 to 24 g. Using by itself, up to 60 g.Samples of formulae:處方舉例 ﹕For hemorrhagic fever:Formula 1:  bai mao gen, da huang , mang xiao, zhi shi  tao ren , sheng di huang , mai dong ,  mu tong  　Fornula 2:  bai mao gen 150 g, dan shen 20 g, lu gen 30 g, huang bai 10 g, pei lan 15 g, dan pi 10 g.Adjust the formulae according to the syndrome.出 血 性 熱 病 :處 方 一 ﹕ 白 茅 根 , 大黃 , 芒 硝, 枳 實   桃仁,  生地黃,  麥 冬 ,   木 通. 處 方 二 ﹕ 白 茅 根 150 g,  丹 參   20 g, 蘆根 30 g, 黃 柏  10 g, 佩蘭 15 g,  丹皮 10 g.　For Cancer of the digestive track:bai mao gen 10 g, bai hua she she cao白花蛇舌草  12 g, ban zhi lian半枝蓮 10 g. Boil with water.
