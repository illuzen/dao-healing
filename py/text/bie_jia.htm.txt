Soft shell turtle shell powder, for oral of 280 mg / kg in mice with transplanted cancer MH134, inhibited tumors with reduced diameter, and significantly reduced tumor weight; but for ascites carcinoma there was no significant effect. On the nude mice with transplanted human colon cancer cells,  given the daily dose of  800mg / kg of soft shell turtle shell powder, the result was that: treatment and control group, the inhibitory rate was 92.15%, and tumor necrosis area was 67%. Compare to using the drug 5-fluorouracil (5- Fu) group, the advantage was that it did not cause white blood cell count to decrease. This indicate that the turtle shell powder can inhibit colorectal cancer in human, and with only small side effect, bone marrow suppression effect is much less than that of 5-Fu.  《source: Zhong Hua Ben Cao 》藥理﹕防 止肝,  脾硬化 (組織壞死 )有 增強身體免疫機能作用 。抗腫瘤作用。鱉甲粉末口服280mg/kg對小鼠移植實質性癌MH134具有抑制作用，使腫瘤直徑減小，腫瘤重量顯著減輕。對腹水癌則沒有顯著作用。對接種人腸癌細胞的裸鼠每日按800mg/kg劑量口服鱉甲粉，治療35 天後與對照組比較，抑瘤率為92.15%，腫瘤壞死面積達67%，與5-氟尿嘧啶(5-Fu)組比較,其優點是不引起宿主白細胞數下降，表明鱉甲粉不僅對人腸癌有抑制作用，且副反應小，對骨髓的抑制遠比5-Fu輕。(資料來源: 《 中華本草 》 Actions & Indications:主治﹕For yinxu (yin deficient)   with flashes: usually combine with qing hao, di gu pi  For yinxu with heat and pain: usually combine with gui ban, mu li, bai shao, e jiao .For chronic malaria, pain in rib and chest, amenorrhea, cirrhosis hardening and swelling of liver and spleen: usually combine with san leng , e zhu, qing pi , xiang fu zi, hong hua  ,tao ren.For enlargement of liver and spleen.For enlargement of thyroid glandsFor enlargement of lymphatic glandsBie jia should be cooked first (cook longer than other herbs) or roasted in sand.In promoting yin and and suppressing yang, bie jia should be use untreated.
