(京大戟)Knoxia valerianoides Thorel ex Pitard (紅 芽大戟)Pron. in Japanese:日語發音﹕taigekiPron. in Korean:韓語發音﹕taegukPron. in Cantonese:粵音﹕daai6 gik1Other Names:別名﹕xia ma xian下馬仙， qiao 蕎， qiong ju 邛巨， long fu cao 龍虎草，qiu tou shi zi cao  九頭獅子草 ，jing da ji 京大戟， jiang jun cao 將軍草， pen zhang cao 膨脹草， tian pin yi zhi xiang 天平一枝香， po shui long 迫水龍，da mao er yan 大貓兒眼， huang hua da ji 黃花大戟 ，huang ya da ji 黃芽大戟 ，qian ceng ta 千層塔， shou shan hu 搜山虎， chuan shan hu 穿山虎。Common Name:英文名﹕Peking spurge rootDistribution:產地﹕Euphorbia pekinensis A Rupr. is mainly produced in Jiangsu, Sichuan, Jiangxi, Guangxi provinces of China.Knoxia valerianoides Thorel ex Pitard is mainly produced in Guangxi, Guangdong, Yunnan, Guizhou provinces of China.京大戟： 主產於江蘇、四川、江西、廣西等地。紅芽大戟：主產於廣西、廣東、雲南、貴州等地。Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕bitter, sweet, very cold and toxic.味甘，苦，大寒，有毒。  Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕kidney, large intestine, lung腎，大腸，肺。Medical functions:藥理﹕  Actions & Indications:主治﹕For edema, ascites, retention of phlegm, tuberculosis of lymph nodes.Can be taken internally or applied externally.治水腫，水臌，痰飲，瘰疬，癰疽腫毒。1,瀉水逐飲：主治水腫脹滿，二便不通，形証俱實者。也可用於痰飲積聚，胸膈脹滿，脅肋隱痛。 2,消腫散結：可用於癰腫瘡毒及痰火瘰癘， 內服。 本品瀉水之力較大，用於水腫、腹脹、腹水、胸水。消腫解毒 用於癰瘡腫毒，可外敷內服。Chemical ingredients:化學成份﹕Root contains triterpene, alkaloids, free anthraquinone, euphobon, euphobia A, B, C. Also contain rubber, resin, 4 types of anthraquinone, and 2 kinds of organic acid: syringic acid and another organic acid whose chemical formula has not been determined.Knoxia valerianoides contains ursolic acid and β-Sitosterol.主要成分：新鮮葉含維生素C。根含三萜、生物鹼、游離蒽醌類及給合性蒽醌類，大戟甙 ，大戟色素體A,B,C等。另含樹膠、樹脂，含4種蒽 醌化合物為3 -羥基巴戟醌；1，3，5，6-四羥基-2-甲基蒽醌、虎刺醛、甲基異茜草素及1，3，5-三羥基-2-甲基-6-甲氧基蒽醌(紅 大戟素)；尚有2種有機酸：丁香酸和另一結構未定的有機酸。紅芽大戟屬含熊果酸(Ursolic acid)(1)，麥胚固醇(β-Sitosterol)。Dosage:用 量﹕Internal use: 1.5 to 3 g in decoction. 1 g per time in powder form.External use: use appropriate amount. Wash affected area with decoction.1.5－3g。散劑每次1g。外用適量，煎水熏洗。Samples of formulae:處方舉 例﹕ Modern Research:現代研究﹕ Toxicity & Cautions:毒素與禁忌﹕Not to use during pregnancy. Not compatible with yuan hua, gan cao.
