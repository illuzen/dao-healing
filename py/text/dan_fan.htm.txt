dan fan (Hydrated Copper Sulfate)  膽礬  
        
        (in English and Chinese 中英文)
by: Joe Hing kwok Chu

Pharmaceutical name:
英文藥名﹕ 
Chemical name:
學名﹕Hydrated Copper SulfatePron. in Japanese:日語發音﹕ Pron. in Korean:韓語發音﹕ Pron. in Cantonese:粵音﹕ Other Names:別名﹕ya chui lu dan fan 鴨嘴綠膽礬Common Name:英文名﹕ChalcanthiteDistribution:產地﹕
          Mainly produced in western part of China.主產於中國西部。Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕sour, acrid, cold and toxic酸﹐辛﹐寒；有毒。
Channels (meridians) entered: 
歸經﹕liver, gall bladder
          肝﹐胆。
Medical functions:藥理﹕ 
            Promotes bile secretion.利膽作用。
Actions & Indications:
主治﹕Internal use: for promoting vomiting, getting rid of excessive wind/cold type of phlegm, toxic material.External use: for drying out wounds, getting rid of rotten part of boils, dislodging wind/cold type of phlegm, sore throat, epilepsy, eating toxic substances, infected eyes, boils in the mouth, infected gum, swollen boils unable to rupture.内服催吐，涌吐風痰，毒物；外用解毒收濕，蝕瘡去腐。用于風痰堵塞，喉痹，癲癇，誤食毒物，風眼赤爛，口瘡，牙疳，腫毒不破等。
