du huo 獨活
 
Edited 


        by: Joe Hing kwok Chu
 




Pharmaceutical name:
英文藥名﹕

Radix Angelicae Pubescentis



Botanical name:
學名﹕

Angelica pubescens Maxim. f. biserrata 
            Shan et Yuan



Pron. in Japanese:
日語發音﹕

dokkatsu



Pron. in Korean:
韓語發音﹕

tokhwal





            Pron. in Cantonese:
粵音﹕

duk6 wut6



Other Names:
別名﹕


xiang du huo 香獨活，da 
            du huo 大獨活。



Common Name:
英文名﹕

doubleteeth pubescent angelica root.



Distribution:
產地﹕

Hubei, Sichuan (A. pubescens); Anhui, Zhejiang, 
            Jiangxi, Hubei, Guangxi  (A.p. Maxim. f. biserrata)



Properties
(characteristics)﹕
性味﹕

slightly warm, acrid, bitter性微温，味辛、苦。




Channels (meridians) entered:
 
歸經﹕

kidney, bladder腎﹐ 膀胱。



Medical functions:
藥理﹕
 



anti-arthritis, inhibits pain, sedative effect
expand blood vessels, lower blood pressure

 

抗關節炎、鎮痛、鎮靜；
擴張血管、降低血壓。





Actions & 
            Indications:
主治﹕

for wind-cold-damp painful obstruction, 
            especially in the lower back and legsfor sickness induced by wind-cold-dampness




Chemical ingredients:
化學成份﹕

1. osthol, eremophilene, thymol, alpha-cedrene, 
            humulelne, p-cresol, beta-cedrene, guaiol, (1-isopropyl-4-methylene-7-methyl-1, 
            2, 3, 4, 4a, 5, 6, 8a-octahydronaphthalene), [2-methyl-4-(1, 1-dimethylethyl) 
            phenol] (dodecyl isopropyl ether), alpha-elemene, (trans-p-menth-2-en-7-ol), 
            (2-methyl-5-, (1, 5-dimethyl-4-hexenyl)-1, 3-cyclohexadiene), alpha-funebrene, 
            alpha-bergamotene, (7, 10-pentadecadiynoic acid), [4, 11, 11-trimethyl-8-methylene-bicyclo 
            (2, 2, 0) undec-4-ene], alpha-bisabolene, alpha-longipinene, alpha-patchoulene, 
            Elixeine, (5, 6-dimethyl-3a, 4, 7, 7alpha-tetrahydro-1, 3-isobenzofurandione), 
            ([Z, E] -4, 8, 12-trimethyl-3, 7, 11-tridecatrienoate), (4, 4-methylenebis 
            [2, 3, 5, 6-tetr-amethyl] phenol), beta-bisabolene, 2-cyclo-hexyl decane,  
            Isosericenene, -2(oxacyclohexadecan-2-one), (2, 3-diphenyl-2-cyclopropen-1-one), 
            (1-tetradecene), (methyl 2, 4, 6-trimethyl-decanoate), n-octane, (4-methyl-cyclohexanone), 
            2-methyloctane, n-nonane, alpha-pinene, alpha-terpinene, 3-methylnonane, 
            beta-pinene, myrcene, alpha-phellandrene, 3-enecarene, p-cymene, sylvestrene, 
            n-undecane, myrtenal, (4-methoxy-acetophenone, (alpha-copaene), beta-elemene, 
            neroliclol.2. angelol, bergapten, osthol, umbelliferone, scopoletin, 
            glabralactone, columbianadin, columbianetin acetate, columbianetin, 
            xanthotoxin, angelol-B.
3. angelic acid, tiglic acid.




Dosage:
用量﹕

normal usage in decoction: 3 to 9 g.external 
            use: make into alcohol extract or into decoction
內服入煎劑，常用量3 ~ 9 克；或浸酒。外用：煎水洗。




Samples of formulae:
處方舉例﹕



du huo ji sheng tang  獨活寄生湯


            jiang huo sheng shi tang 薑活勝濕湯




Modern Research:
現代研究﹕

 



Toxicity & 
            Cautions:
毒素與禁忌﹕

do not use in cases of high fever without 
            chill or in case of
            yinxu (yin deficient)
            with fever.高熱而不惡寒或
陰虛有熱者忌 
            服。
