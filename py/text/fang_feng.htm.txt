Schischk   (synonym )Pron. in Japanese:日語發音﹕bafuPron. in Korean:韓語發音﹕bangp'ungPron. in Cantonese粵音﹕fong4 fung3Other Names:別名﹕ guan fang feng 關防風﹐dong fang feng 東防風。Common Name:英文名﹕ Saposhniovia divaricate rootDistribution:產地﹕Being grown in northern part of China and the Chang Jiang River (Yangtse River) valley.在中國北方及長江流域均有栽培。Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕acrid, sweet, slightly warm.辛﹐甘，微温。Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕bladder, liver, spleen.膀胱﹐肝﹐脾經。Medical functions:藥理﹕ antipyretic inhibits pain antibiotoc antiviral 解熱﹐ 鎮痛 抗菌 抗病毒Actions & Indications:主治﹕For treatment of:blurry vision and headache due to wind chill;stiff neck and pain back;wind cold and damp type of joint and muscle pain;spastic and tight limbs;tetanus;This herb can relieve pain and does not create dryness. 治:外 感 風 寒 之 頭 痛 目 眩 ，脊 痛 項 強 ，風 寒 濕 痺 ，骨 節 酸 痛 ，四 肢 攣 急 ，破 傷 風 ， 祛 風而不燥。 為風藥中之潤 藥 。Chemical ingredients:化學成份﹕fangfengalpyrimidine  , clemiscosin A , 5-hydroxy-8-methoxypsoralen, sec-O-glucosylhamaudol , hamaudol , nodakenetin , prim-O-glucosylcimifugin , cimifugin , 4'-O-beta-D-glucosyl-5-O-methylvisamminol , 5-O-methylvisamminol , marmesin , adenosine , daucosterol ,  beta-sitosterol [1]防風嘧啶(1),clemiscosin A (2),5-羥基-8-甲氧基補骨脂 素(3),亥茅酚苷(4),亥茅酚(5),紫花前胡苷元(6),升麻素苷(7),升麻素(8),5-O-甲基維斯阿米醇苷(9),5-O-甲基維 斯阿米醇(10),異紫花前胡苷(11),腺苷(12),胡蘿卜苷(13)和β-谷甾醇(14)[1]Dosage:用 量﹕For ridding of wind chill: 3－9 g. Larger amount of more than 20 g are being used in muscle spasm.用於外感風寒：3～9 克。用於脊痛項強 ，四肢攣急，有用多過20 克。Samples of formulae:處方 舉例﹕yu ping feng san 玉屏風散sheng yang chu shi fang feng tang 生陽除濕防風湯Modern Research:現代研究﹕ Cautions:禁忌﹕Use care in cases of blood deficient   or yinxu (yin deficient)  with heat and headache..凡血 虛及陰 虛火旺頭痛者慎用。　[1] Yao Xue Xue Bao. 2007 May;42(5):505-10. 藥學學報 ACTA PHARMACEUTICA SINICA，2007年，五月；42(5):505-10. Synonyms:Cachrys seseloides (Hoffm.)
