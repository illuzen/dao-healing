Chinese cucumber seed.Distribution:產地﹕throughout China, especially in Shandong, Hebei, Shanxi, Shaanxi全中國，尤其是山東， 河北，山西，陕西。 Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕faint smell, bland.氣微，味淡。Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕lung, stomach, large instestine肺，胃，大腸。Medical functions:藥理﹕ See tian hua fen page 看天花粉頁 Actions & Indications:主治﹕Being used in hot cough with sticky phlegm; hot and dry bowel with constipation.
