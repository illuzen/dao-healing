    pm. LAH  hai feng teng
       海風藤  
      (in English and Chinese  中英文)
Translated 
by: Joe Hing kwok ChuPharmaceutical name:英文藥名﹕Caulis Piperis kdsuraeBotanical name:拉丁生物學名﹕Piper kadsura (Choisy) OhwiPron. in Japanese:日語發音﹕kaifutoPron. in Korean:韓語發音﹕haepungdeong
Pron. in Cantonese::粵音﹕hoi2 fung1 tang4Other Names:別名﹕man keng xiang滿坑香lao teng老藤da feng teng大風藤yan hu jiao岩胡椒ba yan xiang巴岩香 feng teng 風藤Common Name:英文名﹕kadsura pepper stemsDistribution:產地﹕It is distributed in Zhejiang, Fujian, Guangdong, Taiwan. Mainly produced in Fujian, Zhejiang, and Taiwan.分佈於浙江、福建、廣東、台灣等地。主產福建、浙江及台灣等地。Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕acrid, bitter, slightly warm.辛，苦，微溫。Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕heart, liver, kidney.心，肝，腎。Medical functions:藥理﹕ Effect on coronary heart disease and cerebral thrombosis:Hai feng teng can increase myocardial nutritional blood flow of mice, and reduce myocardial ischemia collateral vascular resistance of dogs, and has a good effect on coronary heart disease and cerebral thrombosis.[1][2]Anti-inflammation effect:[2][3]  Hai feng teng extract can antagonize the rat intravenous endotoxin-induced decline in arterial blood pressure, and reduce endotoxemia pulmonary edema caused by increased pulmonary vascular wall permeability. Such effects of hai feng teng may be related to its antagonistic effect on PAF (platelet activating factor) by its chemical ingredient of futoenone  The traditional Chinese medicine, hai feng teng, is a worthy for trying to treat endotoxin shock. [1]. Denudatin-B showed potent PAF antagonistic activity in 3H-PAF receptor binding assay[3] 海風藤能增加小鼠心肌營養血流量，降低狗心肌缺血區側枝血管阻力，對冠心病和腦血栓有較好的療效。海風藤提取物可以拮抗大鼠靜脈注射內毒素引起的動脈血壓下降，並能減輕內毒素血症對肺血管壁通透性增高引起的肺水腫。海風藤的這種效應可能與其中風藤烯酮與PAF的拮抗作用有關。PAF（血小板激活因子）提示海風藤是一種值得嘗試的治療內毒素休克的中藥。【1】化驗結果玉蘭脂B表現出強有力的拮抗3H-PAF之受體而降低PAF（血小板激活因子）之 活性。[3]Actions & Indications:主治﹕for getting rid of rheumatism, clearing  meridians, correcting qi flow (li qi), arresting pain. Mainly used in wind cold type of arthritis, pain in joints, spastic muscles, cold and pain in abdomen, edema.祛風濕；通經絡；理氣；止痛。主風寒濕痺；肢節疼痛；筋脈拘攣；脘腹冷痛；水腫Chemical ingredients:化學成份﹕ Stocks and leaves contain:莖、葉含:Futoxide細葉青蔞藤索Futoenone細葉青蔞藤烯酮Futoquinol細葉青蔞藤酮醇Futoamide細葉青蔞藤酷胺(風藤酰胺)β-Sitosterolβ-谷甾醇Stigmasterol豆甾醇Evaporating oil:揮發油:α-PineneΑ-蒎烯β-Pineneβ--蒎烯Limonene檸檬烯Sabinene香檜烯Camphene莰烯Isoasarone異細辛醚Futoxide長穗巴豆素，futoenone風藤烯酮kadsurenon海風藤酮licarin里卡靈Denudatin B玉蘭脂Bfutoquinol風藤醌醇α and β-pineneα-及β-蒎烯Cedrene香松烯Isoasarone異細辛醚Also contains:尚含:β-sitosterolβ-谷甾醇stigmasterol豆甾醇recently 4 macrophyllin type of dicyclo [3,2,1] octane type of new lignanoids have been separated.近年又分得4個大葉素型雙環[3,2,1]辛烷類新木脂素。Daly Dosage:每日用量﹕6 -12 gSamples of formulae:處方舉例﹕For postpartum arthritis [2]Name of Formula: Qing Re Chu Bi Tang (Rid of heat and arthritis decoction)Application: Pain in the body, red, hot and swelling in jointsComposition: ren dong teng 30 g, wei ling xian 9 g, qing feng teng 15 g, hai feng teng 15 g, luo shi teng 15 g, fang ji 9 g, sang zhi 30 g, zhui di feng 9 g.Boil with water and use as tea.   方名:  清熱除痺湯 （劉奉伍婦科經驗）功效:  清熱散濕，疏風活血。 主治:  產後身疼，關節紅腫灼痛。組成: 忍冬藤1兩，威靈仙
