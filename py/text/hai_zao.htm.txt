        hai zao 海藻

by: Joe Hing kwok Chu



Pharmaceutical name:
英文藥名﹕

Herb Sargassi



Botanical name:
學名﹕


Sargassum 
            siliquastrum Ag

Sargassum pallidum (Turn.) C. Ag.（大 
            葉海藻)

Sargassum fusiforme (Harv.) Setch. 
            (羊棲菜小葉海藻)




Pron. in Japanese:
日語發音﹕

kaiso



Pron. in Korean:
韓語發音﹕

haecho





            Pron. in Cantonese::
粵音﹕

hoi2 zou2



Other Names:
別名﹕

 



Common Name:
英文名﹕

sea weed



Distribution:
產地﹕

栽培於福建，浙江，廣東，遼寧，山東，臺灣等省。Being cultivated 
            in Fujian, Zhejiang, Guangdong, Liaoning, Shandong, Taiwan provinces.




Properties
(characteristics)﹕
性味﹕

苦，鹹，寒。bitter, salty, cold.




Channels (meridians) entered:
 
歸經﹕

腎，肝，肺，胃。kidney, liver, lung, stomach.



Medical functions:
藥理﹕
 

 



Actions & 
            Indications:
主治﹕

Being used in goiter, scrofula, cough, asthma, 
            haemorrhoids, runny nose, discomfort in digestive tract, pain in hernia, 
            pain and swelling of testes, as diuretics for edema, and as antipyretic.
            用於甲狀腺腫，瘰歷（頸部淋巴腺結核），利尿消腫，退燒、治咳，以及治療氣喘，咳 嗽， 痔瘡、流鼻涕、腸胃不適，疝氣痛，睪丸腫痛，泌 
            尿疾病等




Chemical ingredients:
化學成份﹕

 



Daly Dosage:
每日用量﹕

4.5 - 15 g



Samples of formulae
:處方舉例﹕

 



Modern Research:
現代研究﹕

 




Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕

Not to use in cases of 
pixu (spleen deficient).  
            Some traditional sources mentioned that hai zao is not compatible with
            
gan cao but 
            in clinical application it is being used with
            gan cao.

 脾 
            虛者忌用。傳統認為海 
            藻反甘 
            草，但臨床也每有配伍用者。。
