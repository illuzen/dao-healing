Take orally.治鼻竇炎：以黃水茄根及苦瓜根，水煎服。Modern Research:現代研究﹕ Toxicity & Cautions:毒素與禁忌﹕Huang shui qie is toxic. Overdose can cause dry mouth, thirst , difficulty in swallowing , fever , dry skin, redness , dilated pupils , blurry vision and other symptoms. If poisoning is severe, it can cause respiratory , circulatory inhibition and even respiratory failure and result in death.黃水茄有毒，劑量過大可導致口乾、口渴、吞咽困難、體溫升高、皮膚乾燥發紅、瞳孔擴大、視力模糊等中毒症狀，重者可出現呼吸、循環抑制，甚至呼吸衰竭而致死。[1] National Herbal Compendium 全國中草藥匯編[2] Zhong Hua Ben Cao  中華本草[3] "Pamphlet of Commonly used Chinese Herbs" of Guangzhou Arm Forces,  廣州部隊《常用中草藥手冊》
