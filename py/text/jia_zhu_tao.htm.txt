Cultivated in most parts of China.中國各省區均有栽培。原產伊朗。Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕bitter, cold, toxic.苦，寒，有毒。Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕heart, lung, kidney.心，肺，腎。Medical functions:藥理﹕ Its saponins may be a good potential for use as cardiac stimulant.S.A.A. Jassim and M.A. Naji: exhibited considerable antiviral activities against influenza virus (RNA) and HSV (DNA) (Rajbhandari et al. 2001).其皂素有强心作用，很可能有潜在發展為强心剤。有抗病毒作用：抗流行性感冒病毒與抗單純性疱疹病毒。Actions & Indications:主治﹕Being used in cardiac exhaustion, asthmatic cough, epilepsy, swelling and pain from impact injuries, amenorrhea; but overdose can cause serious arrhythmia, hypotension, and coma.In Ayurvedic medicine, the roots are being used as aphrodisiac, tonic and for chronic pain in abdomen and in joints. The flowers are also being used as aphrodisiac and for lumbago, inflammations, headache and scabies. (Do not use if you are not trained in this herb)強心利尿，祛痰定喘，鎮痛，散瘀止痛作用。。用於 治療心臟衰竭，喘息咳嗽，癲癇，跌打損傷腫痛，經閉。但使用過量會導致嚴重之心律不整，血壓下降，昏迷。在古印度醫學﹐根用 作催情補劑﹐與用來治療關節與腹部慢性痛症。花亦用作催情補劑﹐並用來治療腰部神經痛﹐慢性發炎﹐頭痛﹐與疥瘡。(如非有經驗﹐不可隨意應用此藥。)Chemical ingredients:化學成份﹕洋地黄毒貳元digitoxigenin夾竹桃貳元oleandrigenin烏沙貳元uzarigenin歐夾竹桃貳元adynerigenin奈利貳元nerlagenin歐夾竹桃貳丙adynerigenin C孕菌烯醇酮貳pregnenolone gincoside孕甾烷pregnane歐夾二烯酮Aneridienone  A龍胆二糖gentiobiose夾竹桃貳Aoleandrigenin A糖醇白堅皮醇 L-(＋)-bornesitol橡膠肌醇damhonitol雞蛋花素Plumericin黄酮flavonoids三帖類 化合物   triterpenoids甾醇sterolDosage:用量﹕In decoction for oral consumption: 0.3 to 0.9 g.In powder form: 0.15 g to 0.3 g.煎湯，内服：0.3~0.9 克。作粉剤：0.15 克～0.3克。Samples of formulae:處方舉例﹕ Modern Research:現代研究﹕ Toxic Portions:有毒部份﹕Whole plant is toxic even the smoke from burning the plant.全株有毒。燃燒枝葉之煙霧也有劇毒。Cautions:禁忌﹕It is a very toxic herb.Not to use during pregnancy.
