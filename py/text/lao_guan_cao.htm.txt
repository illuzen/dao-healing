Make into thick decoction. Use as 3 serving per day.for muscle paralysis: lao guan cao, jun gu cao (Herba Ajugae decumbens ), shu jin cao (herba Lycopodiastri casuarinoides) )with meat make into stew use as soup.治急慢性腸炎下痢：牻牛兒苗六錢，紅棗四枚。煎濃湯，一日三回分服。（《現代實用中藥》）治筋骨瘫痪：老鹳草，筋骨草，舒筋草，炖肉服。（《四川药誌》）Modern Research:現代研究﹕It is rich in potential sources of natural antioxidants.
