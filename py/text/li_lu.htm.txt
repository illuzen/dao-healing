The dosage is 0.3 to 0.9 gram.Vomit caused by taking this herb can be stopped by drinkingthe decoction of white portion of green onion.作催吐用。如欲湧吐痰涎，作為急救，可用防風，瓜蒂，藜蘆三味，研細末，取少量，溫水送服以催吐。本品有劇毒，用時宜慎。 用量為﹕0.3 克至0.9 克服本品後吐者服蔥湯即止。Modern Research:現代研究﹕ Toxicity & Cautions:毒素與禁忌﹕Do not take li lu in cases of pregnancies or if the body is weak.The toxic reaction includes: lowering of blood pressure, lowering of heart rate and slowing of respiration rate. The main effect of the alkaloids of li lu is on the nerves of motor nerves, sensory nerves and vagus nerves. and also on the CNS andstriated muscles. It initially stimulates then paralyzes. Death can result due to the paralysis of the CNS of the respiratory system and heart. It antagonizes ren shen, dan shen, sha shen, xuan shen ku shen, xi xin, shao yao and not compatible with da huang and green onion.  身體虛弱及孕婦忌用。本品有劇毒，不宜久服，多服。藜蘆的毒性極大，含毒成分為藜蘆鹼﹐胚芽兒鹼﹐紅藜蘆鹼等。有降低血壓，減慢心率，減慢呼吸的作用。
藜蘆鹼主要作用於運動神經﹐感覺神經及迷走神經，並作用於中樞神經及橫紋肌，使之先興奮後麻痹，可因心臟﹐呼吸中樞麻痹而危及生命。反人參﹐丹參﹐沙參﹐玄參﹐苦參﹐細辛﹐芍藥。惡 大黃，畏蔥白。吐者服蔥湯即止。Symptoms of Toxicity:中毒症狀﹕Toxic reactions include: the sensation of poking by needle in the throatand tongue, burning sensation in the upper abdomen and breast bone,drooping of saliva, nausea, vomiting, pain in abdomen, diarrhea, bloodin stool, numbness around the mouth area, blurry vision, hazy mind,arrhythmia. In serious cases the blood pressure drops, vertigo, headache,difficulty in breathing, whole body spasm and eventually function of theheart and breathing stop.一般中毒舌及咽喉有針刺樣感覺，上腹部及胸骨後有燒灼感，還有流涎﹐惡心﹐嘔吐﹐腹痛 腹瀉﹐血性大便﹐口周圍麻木﹐視覺模糊﹐神志不清﹐心律失常等。嚴重中毒可引起血壓下降﹐眩暈﹐頭痛﹐呼吸困難﹐全身痙攣，最後可至心跳或呼吸停 止。Antidote:解毒治療﹕Lavage with potassium permanganate solution or with 1% tannin acid.
