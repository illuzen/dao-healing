lian qian 
    cao  連錢草 ( 
    in English and Chinese 中英文)


by: Joe Hing kwok Chu 
      
朱興國編 
    譯   




Pharmaceutical name:
英文藥名﹕

herba Glechomae



Botanical name:
拉丁生物學名﹕

Glechoma longituba (Naka) Kupr .



Pron. in Japanese:
日語發音﹕

rensensou （れんせんそう）



Pron. in Korean:
韓語發音﹕

yeongjeoncho; tugolso





        Pron. in Cantonese::
粵音﹕

lin4 cin4 cou2



Other Names:
別名﹕

Common Name:
英文名﹕

long tube ground ivy



Distribution:
產地﹕

mainly produced in provinces Jiangsu, Zhejiang 
        area. Besides Northwestern part and Inner Mongolia, it is found in all parts 
        of China.主産於江蘇，浙江一帶。除西北，內蒙外，中國各地都有。




Properties
characteristics﹕
性味﹕

acrid, slightly bitter, slightly cold.味辛，
        微苦，微寒。




Channels meridians entered:
 
歸經﹕

liver, kidney, bladder.肝，腎，膀胱。



Medical functions:
藥理﹕【1】
 

1.cholagogue (promotes bile secretion),
        2. Diuretic effect, 
        3. Dissolve stones effect: it causes the urine becomes acidic,  the 
        calculus that were under alkaline conditions get to dissove.
        4. Antibacterial test: it is extremely sensitive to Staphylococcus aureus, 
        Shigella sonnei; but it is not sensitive to Escherichia coli, Pseudomonas 
        aeruginosa, Salmonella typhi.1。利膽作用，能促進膽細胞的膽汁分泌，肝膽管內膽汁增加，內壓增高，膽道括紙醉金迷
        肌松弛，而使膽汁排出。
        2。利尿作用，煎劑大鼠灌胃有顯著利尿作用，連續應用則利尿作用逐漸降低。麻醇家兔試驗也有明顯利尿作用。
        3。溶解結石作用，能使小便變為酸性，而使存在於鹼性條件下的結石溶解。
        4。抑菌試驗，對金黃色葡萄球菌極度敏感，宋內痢疾杆菌中離敏感，大腸杆菌、綠膿杆菌、傷寒杆菌均不敏感。




Actions & Indications:[2]
主治﹕【2】

Antipyretic, antitussive, quenching thirst, 
        hemostatic, diuretic. For treatment of pediatric epilepsy; scrofulaceum; 
        grind into juice and use as eye drop for red eye; mix with salt and make 
        into paste to apply to swollen carbuncles and eczema.解熱，鎮咳，止渴，止血，利尿。治
        小兒癇熱，疳病，瘰疬；研汁點暴赤眼；以鹽揉貼腫毒並風癬。

Daly Dosage:
每日用量﹕

15~30 g



Samples of formulae:
處方舉例﹕

For mumps: use fresh herb, pound into paste 
        and apply externally.[2]For rheumatoid arthritis： smash lian qian cao. 
        Stir fry with wine until warm and apply externally. [3]
撐耳寒(腮腺炎)：鮮草搗汁外敷。 [2]
治風濕性關節炎：團經藥(連錢草)，捶絨酒炒熱，外敷。 [3]
