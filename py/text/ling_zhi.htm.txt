ling zhi 靈芝 （in English and Chinese 中英文)By: Joe Hing Kwok ChuPharmaceutical name:英文药名：Ganodermae lucidiGanodermae japoniciBiological name:拉丁文生物學名：Ganoderma lucidum or Ganoderma japonicumPron. in Japanese:日語發音：mannentake(萬年茸); reishi （灵芝）Pron. in Korean:韓語發音：yung cheePron. in Cantonese:粤語發音：leng4 zi1Other names:別名：mu ling zhi 木靈芝，jun ling zhi 菌靈芝，wan nian xun 萬年蕈，ling zhi cao 靈芝草，chi zhi 赤芝，hong zhi 紅芝。Common Name:英文普通名：Lucid ganodermaDistribution:分佈：Red lucid ganoderma is produced in East China, Southwest China, Hebei, Shanxi, Jiangxi, Guangxi and Guangdong provinces. Violet lucid ganoderma is produced in Zhejiang, Jiangxi, Hunan, Guangxi, Fujian and Guangdong provinces.赤芝産於中國東部，西南部：河北，山西，江西，廣西，廣東等省。紫芝産 於浙江，江西，湖南，廣西，福建，廣東等省。Properties (characteristics)：性味：Sweet, neutral.甘，平。Channels (meridians) entered:歸經：Lung, Kidney, Liver, Heart, Spleen, Stomach.肺，腎，肝，心，脾，胃。Actions & Indications:主治：Tonic for lung and kidney. For syndrome caused by  both lung qi deficient  and shenxu (kidney deficient)  including cough and asthma which become worse on exertion, shortness of breath and fatigue.Tonic for liver and kidney. For syndrome caused by  both the liver and shenxu (kidney deficient) , such as tinnitus, deafness and lassitude of the loins and knees.For calming anxiety and xinqixu (heart qi deficient)   that resulting in palpitation, insomnia and amnesia.For pixu (spleen deficient)  with weak digestion.For treatment of hepatitis B.For treatment of large B cell lymphoma [3]　作補腎與補肺藥。治肺氣虛與腎虛引起的咳嗽，哮喘而疲 劳時更為嚴重，氣短，疲倦。作為補肝與補腎藥。治肝虚與腎虛所引起的耳鳴，耳聾，腰膝無力。治焦慮。治心氣虛引起的心陣跳，失眠，健忘。脾虛消化不良。治乙型肝炎。治大細胞淋巴癌【3】Medical Function:藥理：effect of Inhibiting replicating of hepatitis B virus.
