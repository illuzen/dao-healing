Medical functions:
藥理﹕【1】
 



    Its function is similar to she xiang. A small amount can excite the central 
    nervous system; and large amount can inhibit CNS. and have the effect of 
    dropping blood pressure.
     龍涎香與麝香相似，小量對動物中樞神經系統有興奮作用，大量則表現抑制；對離體動物的心臟有強心作用，能引起血壓下降。
