ma bao 馬寶
 
Edited 


        by: Joe Hing kwok Chu




Pharmaceutical name:
英文藥名﹕

Calculus Equi



Botanical name:
學名﹕

Equus caballus 
            L 



Pron. in Japanese:
日語發音﹕

 



Pron. in Korean:
韓語發音﹕

 





            Pron. in Cantonese:
粵音﹕

maa5 bou2



Other Names:
別名 ﹕

zha 
            da鲊荅，ma jie shi 馬結石。
Presciption names: ma bao 馬寶,  
            ma bao fen 馬寶粉。
處方名：馬寶，馬寶粉。




Common Name:
英文名﹕

horse bezoar



Distribution:
產地﹕

The 
            herb is mainly produced in Hebei, Inner Mongolia, Liaoning, Jilin, Heilongjiang, 
            Xinjiang, Gansu, Yunan, Guizhou, Xizang (Tibet) of China.
藥材主產河北，內蒙古，遼寧，吉林，黑龍江，新疆，甘肅，雲南，貴州，西藏等地。




Properties
(characteristics)﹕
性味﹕

cold, 
            salty, slightly bitter and slightly toxic.
性涼，味咸微苦，有小毒。




Channels (meridians) entered:
 
歸經﹕

heart, liver.
入心，肝二經。



Medical functions:
藥理﹕
 

 



Actions & 
            Indications:
主治﹕

For 
            treatment of epilepsy, 

yinxu (yin deficient), hot phlegm, 
            mania, delusion and losing consciousness, nose bleeding, vomiting blood, 
            or boils.治驚癇癲狂，陰
            虚痰熱， 神志昏迷，吐血衄血，惡瘡腫毒。




Chemical ingredients:
化學成份﹕

contains
            struvite (ammonium magnesium phosphate, 
            NH4MgPO4.6H2O), 
            dittmarite (NH4MgPO4. H2O),magnesium carbonate, and 
            calcium carbonate.含鳥 
            糞石，磷酸鎂銨，碳酸鎂，碳酸鈣等。




Dosage:
用量﹕

to 
            take orally: in powder form, 0.7 to 2 g.
內服：研末，2～5分。




Samples of formulae:
處方舉例﹕



Name of formula: diang kuang ma 
            bao san 
Application: 
            for treatment of madness, manic anxiety, delirium, mania caused by hot 
            phlegm (symptoms include high fever, delirium, even epilepsy and resulting 
            in mania), spitting saliva, talking without making sense, fright and 
            insomnia, crying and laughing, and schizophrenia. 
Composition: 
            ma bao 30 g, hu po 
            3 g, long xian 
            xiang 1g,
            zhen zhu（bake 
            with tofu）0.3 g,
            niu huang
            
           1.5 g
Grind 
            all herb into fine powder. Bottle 1 g each.
Dosage: 
            take half bottle each time (0.5 g). If ilness is serious, take 1 bottle 
            (1 g)

藥方名：癲狂馬寶散

功能主冶：鎮靜，豁痰，安神，定志。主神經錯亂， 
            狂躁不安。痰熱蒙蔽心竅，症見高熱、神昏、譫語，甚或痙厥等引起的癲狂，口吐涎沫，神識不清，語言譫妄，瘋狂打鬧，煩躁不安，驚惕失眠，哭笑無常
            ，及精神分裂症等。

組成：馬寶 2兩，琥
            珀 1錢，龍
            涎香 1錢，珍
            珠（豆腐炙）1分，牛黃
5分。
製法：上 
            研細末，裝瓶重3分。
            用法用量：每次病輕者服半瓶 (一分半）， 
            病重者服1瓶 (3分)。
 




Modern Research:
現代研究﹕

 



Warning, oxicity 
            & Cautions:
注意，毒素與禁忌﹕

Not 
            to use in cases of
            cold stomach 
            or in damp phlegm.
Warning: there are fake ma bao 
            in the market. See
            
            this study.

胃寒 
痰濕者忌用。
注意：市場上有偽馬 
            寶。有些是水泥做成的。有些是用其他獸類的胃結石代替的。 真馬寶的主要成份是含磷酸鎂銨而假馬寶主要成份是碳酸鈣。

Note:

Yinxu with hot phlegm syndrome: cough 
        with sticky white or yellow phlegm, may even with blood in phlegm, achy 
        and tight sensation in chest area, sometimes with low grade fever, listless 
        sleep, red tongue with thin yellow fur, slippery and thin pulse.
 
陰虚痰 熱主 
        要症候：咳 嗽，咯吐白黏痰或黃稠痰，甚或痰中帶血，胸脅悶痛 ，時有低熱，夜眠不安，舌紅，苔薄黃，脈細滑數。
 
Damp phlegm 
        syndrome: cough with abundance of phlegm, fullness in chest are, nausea, 
        vomiting, dizziness, palpitation.
 
濕痰主 要症
        候：咳 嗽痰多，胸膈脹滿，嘔吐惡心，或暈眩心悸。
 
Cold 
        stomach syndrome: stomach ache due to weather becoming cold, or the 
        body getting chill, or eating cold food, feeling cold in stomach while having 
        pain, pain become less if getting warmth.
