ma bo 馬勃

         


by: Joe Hing kwok Chu






    Pharmaceutical name:

    英文藥名﹕


Fructificatio Lasiosphaerae seu Calvatiae




Botanical name:

    學名﹕


Lasiosphaera fenzlii Reich.、脫皮馬勃

Calvatia gigantea（Batsch ex 
    Pers.）Lloyd
        大馬勃
Calvatia Lilacina（Mont.et Berk.）Lloyd
        紫色馬勃



Pron. in Japanese:
日語發音﹕


babotsu



Pron. in Korean:
韓語發音﹕


mabal



Pron. in Cantonese:
粵音﹕


ma po




Other Names:

別名﹕


nan ma bo 南馬勃﹐zhi ma bo 止馬勃.



Common Name:
英文名﹕


Pearly puffball, warted puffball  




Distribution:

產地﹕


Inner Mongolia, Gansu, Guangdong, Guangxi provinces of China.

內蒙、東北、甘肅、廣東、廣西等地。



Properties
(characteristics)﹕
性味﹕


acrid, neutral.

辛﹐平。




Channels (meridians)
      entered:
 


    歸經﹕


lung.

肺經。




        Medical functions:

        藥理﹕ 


hemostatic effect: external application stops 
            nose or mouth bleeding
antibiotic 
            effect: inhibits bacteria in vitro including Stapylococcus aureus 
            金黃色葡萄球菌 , Pseudomonas aeruginosa 綠膿桿菌 ,  and Streptococcus 
            pneumoniae ，肺炎鏈球菌 .




Actions & Indications:

    主治﹕


for heat in the lungs and pain in the throat, 
        coarse voice. External use: nose bleeding and bleeding due to injuries.

清肺利咽，止血。用於風熱郁肺咽痛，咳嗽，音啞。外治鼻衄，創傷出血。





    Chemical ingredients:

    化學成份﹕

 




Dosage:

用量﹕



1.5 g to 6 g. 

Use appropriate amount for external application.

 

1.5～6g；

外用適量，敷患处。






Samples 
    of formulae:
處方舉例﹕


yin qiao san 銀翹散
 




Modern Research:


    現代研究﹕

 




Toxicity & Cautions:

毒素與禁忌﹕


Not to use when loss of voice due to wind cold.

Allergic reaction: 
    dizziness, throat feels swollen and obstructed, 

    heaviness in chest, itchiness and rash.

風 寒 失 聲 忌 用 。 
    過敏反應包括：頭暈，喉嚨感覺發腫阻塞，胸悶，皮膚發痒，發疹。
