Smash and apply.內服：煎湯，9-15g。外用：適量，搗敷。Samples of formulae:處方舉 例﹕Vaginal bleeding, vaginal discharge: use a handful of mu tou hui, a pinch of new hong hua and water and rice wine, half of each. Cook and simmer down to 70%. Drink before bed time. Use while warm. If sickness is new use one dose. If it is chronic, use 3 doses.
