(Commercial name: jing san leng 荊 三 棱;  )Scirpus yagara Ohwi (Commercial name: hei san leng, 黑 三 棱 )Pron. in Japanese:日語發音﹕sanryoPron. in Korean:韓語發音﹕samnungPron. in Cantonese:粵音﹕sam lengOther Names:別名﹕prescription names: jing san leng 荆三棱, san leng  三棱, hei san leng  黑三棱, cu san leng  醋三棱, zhi san leng  炙三棱﹐jing san leng 京三稜，ban tian dan dan 半天丹丹，xiao jian du wei 削尖都尉。。(因 古时產于荆楚地域，此 藥 材 習 慣 稱 為 �荆三棱�。 )Common Name:英文名﹕bur-reed rhizome, scirpusDistribution:產地﹕Hebei, Liaoning, Jiangxi, Jiangsu.河北, 遼 寧 , 江西, 江 蘇 等地，Properties(characteristics)﹕性味﹕acrid, bitter, neutral辛,苦,平 。Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕liver, spleen.入肝, 脾經 。 Medical functions:    藥理﹕           its decoctioncan intensify the the contraction and tension in vitro intestine of rabbitcan stimulate the in vitro uterus of rabbitcan inhibit the platelet coagulation of rats,can extend the time of thrombosis, reducing the time of blood clots. The inhibition of thrombosis, lowering the  blood viscosity was stronger than the  inhibition of platelet aggregation其煎劑能引起對家兔 離體小腸收縮加強，緊張性升高。對離體兔子宮也有興奮作用。對大白鼠血液凝固的影響：有抑制血小板聚集，延長血栓形成時間，縮短血栓。其抑制血栓形成，降低全血 粘度的作用強於本品對血小板聚集功能的抑 制Actions & Indications:主治﹕painful menses. postpartum complication, amenorrhea cause by qi stagnation and/or blood stasis.  cu san leng (prepared with vinegar) is stronger in pain inhibition.fu san leng (stir fried with flour) is milder and is being used in promoting digestion. 月經痛，產後症候群， 氣滯血 淤所致之閉 經。醋制三棱 ，引藥入肝，增强破血軟堅和止痛作用。麸炒三 棱，性較緩 ， 助消食積 。Chemical ingredients:化學成份﹕The rhizome contains evaportion oil 0.5%三棱塊莖含揮發油 0.5%benzeneethanol苯乙醇1,4-benzenediol對苯二酚hexadecanoic acid十六酸dehydrocostuslactone去氫木香內酯3,4-dihydro-8-hydroxy-3-methyl-1H-2-benzopyran-4-one3，4-二 氫-8-羥基-3-甲基-1H2- 苯並吡喃-4-酮1-hydroxy-2-acetyl-4-methylbenzene1-羥 基-2-乙酰基-4-甲基苯β-elemeneβ欖香烯2- furanmethanol 2-呋 喃醇2-acetylpyrrole2-乙酰基吡咯Organic acid:含多種有機酸：succinic acid琥珀酸sanleng acid三棱酸9-11- octadedicenoic acid9-11-十八碳二烯酸9-12-octadedicenoic acid9-12-十 八碳二烯酸10- nonadecenoic acid)10-十九烯酸11-eicosenoic acid11-二十 烯酸benzoic acid苯甲酸3-phenyl-2-propenoic acid3-苯-2-丙烯酸azelaic acid壬二酸decanedioic acid癸二酸Fatty acid:脂肪酸：contain: fatty acid of C8-C10，C12，C14-C20含有C8-C10，C12，C14-C20的 脂肪酸Also contains:還含：formonetin刺芒柄花素stigmasterol豆甾醇β- sitosterolβ-谷甾醇daucosterol胡蘿卜甙Dosage:用量﹕3 to 9 g in decoction.3-9克，煎服。    Samples of formulae:處方舉例﹕For indigestion and flatulence: san leng 9 g, lai fu zi 9 g.
