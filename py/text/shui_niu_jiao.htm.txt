It is not suitable for bleeding due topixu (spleen deficient) .治熱病頭痛，發 高 熱 神昏，斑疹，吐血 ﹐ 鼻衄，小兒驚風，喉痺咽腫。用 水 研 磨 ﹐外 敷 治 皮 膚 過 敏 反 應 。水牛角適 用 於 熱 盛 而迫血妄行的皮下血斑等多種 出血。如果是因脾虛，脾不统血的皮下血斑，牙龈 出血者，則 不 可 用水牛角止血。Chemical ingredients:化學成份﹕Cholesterol膽甾醇Amino acids氨基酸:Alanine丙氨酸Arginine精氨酸Aspartic acid天冬氨酸Cystine胱氨酸Leucine亮氨酸Proline脯氨酸Tyrosine酪氨酸Histidine組氨酸Valine纈氨酸Peptide肽類serine絲氨酸glycine甘氨酸lysine賴氨酸arginine精氨酸threonine蘇氨酸glutamic acid谷氨酸methionine蛋氨酸isoleucine異亮氨酸phenylalanine苯丙氨酸Dosage:用量﹕Internal consumption with grinding with water: 6 to 15 g.In decoction: 30 to 120 g, orMake into pill or powder formExternal use: grind with water and apply to affected areas. 内服: 磨汁或研末，6～15 克 ；煎湯 ，30 ~ 120 克 ；或人丸，散。外用: 磨汁塗 患 處 。Samples of formulae:處方舉例﹕xi jiao di huang tang (shui niu jiao can be used instead of xi jiao)犀角地黃湯  (水牛角可以用來代替犀角)Modern Research:現代研究﹕ Toxicity & Cautions:毒素與禁忌﹕A small number of patients, after taking this herb, showed discomfort of the stomach, flatulence, diarrhea, nausea, or insomnia.Not to use during pregnancy. Not to use where there is no heat orin cases of pixu (spleen deficient) .少数病人服用後 出現 胃部不 適 ，腹 部 脹 ，腹瀉 ，惡 心 等。亦有少数病人出现失眠。孕婦慎用。 非實 熱 者不宜用。脾 虛者不宜使用。Note: this herb is being used as substitute for rhino horn, which is illegal for commerce.註： 水牛角現用來代替犀牛角用。現犀牛角被禁止買賣。Sponsors' Ads by Google 以 下為谷歌所提供之廣告  A manuscript... writing, translating and proofreading                          in progressClick here for comment.
