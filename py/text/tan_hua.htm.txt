Pron. in Japanese:

 gekkabejin

Other Names:

qiong hua 瓊花，feng hua 鳳花，jin gou lian 金鉤

            美人。







Common Name:

 Dutchman's 
            pipe [1], Dutchman's pipe cactus [2]





Distribution:
產地﹕


All over China
            中國各地












Properties
(characteristics)﹕
性味﹕



neutral, bland, slightly 
            cold.平，淡，微寒。









Channels (meridians) entered:

heart, lung.心，肺。










Medical functions:
藥理﹕






 


  






Actions 
            & Indications:
主治﹕










For treatment of cough due to heat in the lung, consumption, 
            spitting out blood, bleeding not during menses, palpitation, imsomnia.


            清肺止咳，凉血止血，養心安神。主肺熱咳 
            嗽；肺痨；咯血；崩漏；心悸；失眠。
