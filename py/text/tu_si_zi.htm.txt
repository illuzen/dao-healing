　tu si zi   菟絲子by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu　

Pharmaceutical name:英文药名：Semen Cuscutae ChinensisBiological name:拉丁名：Cuscuta chinensis Lam.,C. japonica ChoisyPron. in Japanese:日語發音：toshishiPron. in Korean:8韩語發音：tosachaPron. in Cantonese: 粤語發音：tou3  si1  zi2Common Name:英文名：cuscuta, Chinese dodder seedDistribution:分佈：Jiangsu, Liaoning, Jiling, Hebei, shandong, Henan provinces of China.江蘇、遼寧、吉林、河北、山東、河南省等地區。Properties (characteristics)：性味：acrid, sweet, neutral辛，甘，平。Channels (meridians) entered:歸經：kidney, liver肝，腎Actions & Indications:主治:tonifies kidneys, liver and spleen, improve yin; for shenyangxu, pixu, neurasthenia, calming fetus, for habitual miscarriage.口苦躁渴，陽痿，遺精，血尿，小便淋瀝，大便溏薄，神經衰弱，習慣性流產。Medical Function:To be editedAnti-aging effect. [1]Hepatoprotective effects: tu si zi20% water decoction on carbon tetrachloride injury in mice: providing orally 50g crude drug / kg body weight, can lower the blood lactate, pyruvate, and SGPT decline, and can increase glycogen adrenal ascorbic acid significantly, and shows the protective activity of damaged liver. [2]  Yang and enhance sexual vitality role: 20% the tu si zi water decoction of 0.5ml /  day orally, corrected yang deficiency symptoms in mice. Culture medium containing the tu si zi water decoction of three concentration 0.5,1. O and 2.O% can enhance the drosophila (fruit flies) sexual vitality. [2]  Increase the role of non-specific resistance: tu si zi water decoction can extend swimming time of mice, enhanced hypoxic tolerance under normal pressure, improve its non-specific resistance. [2]  Other role: tu si zi: anti-tumor, anti-viral, anti-inflammatory, anti-infertility, lapactic, and suppress the central nervous system. [2]具有延緩衰老作用。[1]保肝作用：菟絲子20%的水煎劑給四氯化碳損傷小鼠灌胃，50g生藥/kg體重，能使血液中增加的乳酸、丙酮酸及 SGPT 下降，而使下降的肝糖元和腎上腺抗壞血酸上升，有顯著的保護肝損傷活性。[2]  助陽和增強性活力作用：20%菟絲子水煎劑 0.5ml/隻/天 灌胃，對陽虛小鼠的症狀有一定的恢復作用。用含菟絲子水煎劑的培養基培養，在0.5、1.O和2.O%三個濃度下均能提高果蠅的性活力。[2]  增加非特異性抵抗力作用：菟絲子水煎劑，能延氏小鼠負重游泳時間，增強小鼠在常壓下的耐缺氧能力，提高其非特異性抵抗力。[2]  其它作用：菟絲子尚具有抗腫瘤、抗病毒、抗炎、抗不育、致瀉、及抑制中樞神經系統的作用。[2]Chemical ingredients:化学成分：to be loadedDosage:9 to 15 g.Samples of formulae:

For treatment of threatened abortion: Prescription:  tu zi zi , xu duan, e jiao, dang shen, bai zhu. Treated 110 patients. Take herb once a day for 10 days. Effective rate: 96.36%

治療先兆流產的作用：菟絲子與續 斷、阿膠、黨 參、白 朮等配伍，治療先兆流產110例，病人每日服藥一次，共10天，總有效率為96.36%。[1]

治陽痿：菟絲子、蛇床子、五味子各等分。上三味，末之，蜜丸如梧子。飲服三十丸，日三。《方源：千金方 》
