Simmer till 200 c.c. is left. Make porridge with rice and da zao. When the porridge is ready add the strain liquid from xian he cao and bring to boil.Make into 2 servings.  Take every day for 1 to 2 months.  Formula 2 (source: (Middle Level Medical Journal, 1988, Aug issue.xian he cao 60 g. Add water 500 ml. Simmer to 300 ml. Take orally morning and night. 7 to 10 days is one therapy session.採用仙鶴草治療癌，應使用地上全草，去掉根部，每次120克(洗淨)，大 棗 100枚，粳米(或 糯米)100克。將仙鶴草入砂鍋中煎取濃汁500毫升，待粳米與大棗煮粥快成時，兌入仙鶴草汁，者沸後即可食用，每日2次，連服1�2個月。摘自《上海中醫藥報》用草藥仙鶴草60g加水500ml，煎至300ml，每晚1次口服，7～10天為1個療程(摘自《中級醫刊》1988年8月Pharmaceutical name:英 文藥名﹕Herba Agrimoniae
