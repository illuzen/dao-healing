xu duan 
        續斷      
 (in English and Chinese 中英文)
 


        by: Joe Hing kwok Chu



Present Day Appication:
            現 代 應 用 ﹕



miscarriage with bleeding.
treatment of soft tissue injuries

 

治療先兆性 流產。
治療軟組織損傷。





Pharmaceutical Name:
英文藥名﹕

Dipsaci Radix



Botanical name:
學名﹕

Dipsacus asperoides, C. Y.Cheng et 
            TM Ai



Pron. in Japanese:
日語發音﹕

zokudan



Pron. in Korean:
韓語發音﹕

            Pron. in Cantonese:

Common Name:
英文名﹕

dipsacus



Distribution:
產地﹕

middle and southern parts of China.分 
            佈 華中﹐華南等地。




Properties
(characteristics)﹕
性味﹕

bitter, 
            acrid, slightly warm.
味苦、辛，性微溫。




Channels (meridians) entered:
 
歸經﹕

liver, kidneys.入肝、腎二經。



Medical functions:
藥理﹕
 



inhibits and reverse osteoporosis, 
promotes healing of injured bones
alleviates the contraction of the uterus, thus calming the fetus
improves immune system
inhibits diplococcus pneumoniae 
protects from lacking of vitamin E
kills
              
              Trichomoniasis 
helps uterus to grow and develop: helped the uteri of mice to 
              continue to grow and devlop after removal of their ovaries.

 

抗骨 質 疏鬆作用:  川續斷 水煎液對去卵巢大鼠之骨 質疏鬆,可以使之逆轉
              。
促進骨損 傷 愈合  
對子宫的作用﹕對在體 子宫的作用：，降低其收缩幅度和張力；安胎作用。
对免疫功能的影响  川续断水煎液能提高小鼠缺氧能力，延長小鼠负重游泳持 續 
              時間，促進小鼠巨噬细胞的吞噬功能。
對肺炎雙球菌有抑制作用
抗维生素E缺乏症，



              殺滅陰道毛滴虫（ 
              濃 度 為 0.4mg/ml）的作用﹐
促進子宮發育﹕促進去卵巢小鼠子宮生長發育。
