Medical functions:

藥理﹕
 



Ya ma ren (flax seed) and 
            its root contain large amount of lignan which is a strong anti-oxidant 
            and can inhibit the production of  harmful carcinogenic substances.

Anti-cancer of the intestine 
            effect:: ya ma ren (flax seed) contains large amount of soluble fiber 
            which can reduce the toxicity of the digested substances and speed up 
            the secretion of the feces, thus reducing the duration of the time of 
            stimulating substance on the mucous membrane of the intestine.

Ya ma root can help the 
            function of the kidneys to excrete toxins.

Ya ma root contains large 
            amount of omega -3 fatty acid which can lower blood lipids and lower 
            the LDL in the blood and can reduce arteriosclerosis.

Ya ma inhibits the growth 
            of the prostate cells. In vitro studies showed that the lignan of 
            the ya ma ren (flax seed) can inhibit the growth of the cells of the 
            prostate gland itself.

亞麻和
            其根部含大量的木酚素 (lignan) 。具有很强的抗氧化特性，能有效遏
            制促使癌生成的有害化學物 質。

對防治
            腸癌的作用﹕富含纤维素，可减輕腸道中消化物產生的不良影响﹐加快腸道中廢物的排泄，减 短對腸内黏膜有剌激性的物質在腸道中停留的时间。

亞麻根
            有助于腎臟的排毒功能 。

亞麻根
            富含的脂肪酸，可降低血脂，低血液中的低密度胆固醇 (LDL)﹐治疗血管硬化。


控制攝護腺細胞生長。 體外的實驗證明
亞麻仁木酚素(lignan)可 
            以控制攝護腺細胞本身之生長。

            Uses:

Improves bowel movement



Lowers blood lipids.



Seed must be ground for 
              consumption because of the tough seed coat that is indigestible. The 
              ground-up seeds should be taken with water since the mucilage absorbs 
              5 times the weight of the seed.





It is not advisable to 
              use flax seed in baked food since the fatty acid omega-3 cannot withstand 
              high heat and can become carcinogenic.

            contain 75 times the quantity of  Lignan compared to the other 
            plants that contain next highest source of Plant Lignan. The majority 
            of the Plant lignan component of flax seeds is located in the outer 
            husk of the Flax Seed .

Cautions:

禁忌﹕



Omega-3 is a highly unstable 
            fat and can become rancid quickly and become carcinogenic. Do not use 
            flax seed oil for deep fat frying because omega-3 cannot stand high 
            heat. Store flax seed and flax oil in black container and refrigerate. 
            Discard seed or oil that smells fishy or rancid, because that smell 
            signals toxicity.

Seed should be washed before 
            using to avoid the mold , Aspergillus niger,  which is toxic 
            for the respiration system and the mold , Aspergillus flavus,  
            which is toxic for the liver.  These molds are commonly found in 
            grains and are extremely toxic.




        　


Linamarin, after hydrolysis, can release free hydrogen 
        cyanide acid, which can suffocate and poison the cells.

亞麻苦甙（linamarin） ，經水解後可析出游
        離態的氢氰酸，致組織细胞窒息中毒。
 

A diet rich in flax seed seems to reduce the size, aggressiveness and severity 
        of tumors in mice that have been genetically engineered to develop prostate 
        cancer, according to new research from Duke University Medical Center. And 
        in 3 percent of the mice the flax seed diet kept them from getting the disease 
        at all. (From news release issued by Duke 
        University Medical Center).
