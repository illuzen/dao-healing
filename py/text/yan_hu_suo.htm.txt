It only showed sedation and hypnotic condition. After 2 weeks of continuous application the hearts and kidneys showed slightly swollen and fuzzy.給猴一次灌胃乙素85或110mg/kg,或 皮下注射80mg/kg,均未見明顯毒性,僅仅有鎮靜﹐催眠現象。連用两周後作病理切片檢查,發現心﹐腎有輕度濁腫。Cautions:禁忌﹕not to use in cases of heat in blood and qi deficient, or those with early arrival of menses or during pregnancy. Long term usage can be toxic to the heart and kidneys.血熱氣虛者，經水先期者 及孕婦禁用。長期服用可能對心臟與腎臟有毒。Do not use with chlorpromazine hydrochloride. Both possess the effect of sedating the CNS but using together can result in tremor and numbness.Do not use with CNS stimulants like caffein, like phenylalanine because the ingredient of yan hu suo, d1-tetrahydropalmatine possesses sedative effect and can mitigate the effect of the stimulants. Do not use with monoamine oxidase because the hypotensive effect of the effective ingredient of palmatine can be mitigated by the monoamine oxidase like Pargylini Hydrochloridam.Do not use with strychnine or ma qian zi because it can increase their toxicity.1．不宜與氯丙嗪同用二者有類似的安定和中樞性止嘔作用，但同用會產生震顫麻痹。2．不宜與咖啡因等、苯丙胺中樞興奮劑同用延胡索乙素具有中樞抑制作用，會降低西藥中樞興奮劑的藥效。3．不宜與單胺氧化酶抑制劑同用 延胡索的有效成分巴馬汀，其降壓作用可被單胺氧化酶抑制劑如鹽酸優降 寧等所逆轉或消除，故在應用單胺氧化　抑制劑期間及停藥時間不足兩周者，不 宜應用元胡及其制劑。4．不宜與士的寧及馬錢子同用 元胡可增強士的寧的毒性反應。 References: Zhejiang Chinese Medical School.  Yan Hu参考：浙江中醫學院。元胡。Sponsors' Ads by Google 一下為谷歌所提供之廣告  A manuscript... writing, translating and proofreading              in progressClick here for comment.
