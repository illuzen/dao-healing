There was no improvement of mortality and liver disease of mice with viral hepatitis. Yin chen hao tang decoction can promote regeneration effect on liver cells of rabbits.[1] 　　解熱　抗微生物　利膽作用　降壓作用　利尿作用　病毒性肝炎之肝臟：茵陳煎劑及茵陳揮發油對四氯化碳中毒性肝炎家兔的血清轉氨 酶無影響，但後者能使肝炎家兔食量增加，對病毒性肝炎之小白鼠無改善其死亡率及肝細胞病變的作用。茵陳蒿湯煎劑對家兔有促進肝細胞再生作用。[1]Chemical Ingredients:化學成份:1. 香豆素類： 6, 7-dimethoxycoumarin, scoparo ne[1], scopeletin[2],6-hydroxy-7-methylesculetin[3], capilarin[4].2. 色原酮類： capillarisin, 7-methylcapillarisin[5], 4'-methylcapillarisin, 6-demethoxycapillarisin, 6-demethoxy-4'-methyl-capillarisin[6].3. 黃酮類：areapillin[1], isoareapillin[8], cirsimauitin, cirsilineol, genkwanin[6], isorhamnetin, quercetin[9], rhamnocitrin[6], eupatolitin, hyperin, cacticin, isorhamnetin-e-glucoside[9], eupalitin, [3], 7-methyl-aromadendrin[4], rutin, quercetin-3-glucogalactoside, quercetin-3, 7-rutinosodigalactoside, kaempferol-e-glucogalactoside[16].4. 香豆酸與其他有機酸類：. capillartemisin A, B;
