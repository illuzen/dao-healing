zhi shi 枳
        實，zhi ke 枳殼，Jie 
        shi 桔實   



        by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu  (in English 
        and Chinese 中英文)
zhi shi is the immature 
        fruit of citrus aurantium and other varieties 
        while zhi ke is the mature fruit.
