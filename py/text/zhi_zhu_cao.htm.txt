            name:

            Peliosanthes macrophyllaeRadix

Peliosanthes macrophylla Wall. ex 
            Baker



Pron. in Japanese:

zi1 zyu1 cou2



Other Names:

da ye qui zi cao (large leave ball 
            grass) 大葉球子草，大葉入地蜈蚣。



Common Name:
外文名﹕

 



Distribution:
產地﹕

E. Himalaya 
            (Nepal, Sikkim), Assam and Guangdong, Guangxi provinces of 
            China.喜馬拉雅山以東地帶（尼泊爾，錫金），阿薩姆邦，中國的廣東，廣西省。



Properties

sweet, bland, neutral, slightly warm

cough with thick phlegm, chest pain,

            children.主咳嗽痰稠，胸痛，跌打胸脅痛，小兒疳積。

fresh herb 3 to 9 g. Use less on small
