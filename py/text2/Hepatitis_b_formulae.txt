For treatment of non-infectious type of meningitis，hepatitis B, and enterovirus type 71 (EV71). Prescription: jiang can 9～15g, chan tui 8～10 g, da huang 6～15 g , jiang huang 9 g (make into powder and put in during the last minute). Add 8 00 cc of water. Simmer till 32 0 cc is left.
