Heavy Menstrual Bleeding (M enorrhagia ) 月經過多 Translated b y: Joe Hing Kwok Chu (in English and Chinese) Formula for heavy bleeding Formula I Name of Formula: Ping Gan Kai Yu Zhi Xue Fang (Rid of Depression and Arrest Bleeding Formula) 《source of formula: Fu Qing Zhu Gynaecology 》 Application: for liver qi stagnation , with heat in blood Composition: bai shao 30 g, bai zhu 30 g, dang gui 30 g, dan pi 9 , san qi 9 g, sheng di huang 9 g, gan cao 6 g, jing jie 6 g, chai hu 3 g. Boil with water. One dose per day. Formula II Composition: ai ye 2 qian, dang gui 3 qian, bai shao 3 qian, sheng_di huang 3 qian, chuan xiong 1 qian. Note: 1 qian = 3.75 g (old measurement) Boil with three bowls of water. Simmer till one bowl. Make into 3 servings.
