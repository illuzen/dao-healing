Improves memory that has been damaged by drug or alcohol. Adjusts the blood flow of the CNS. Increase s the flow volume of oxygen-carrying blood to the heart muscle ; thus protect s the muscle from damage caused by lack of oxygen. Anti-hypoxia: It may prevent hypoxia-induced biological changes by either increasing intracellular oxygen diffusion and efficiency of oxygen utilization or reducing hypoxia-induced oxidative damage with its anti-oxidative ability ; thus p revent s high altitude sickness . 1 Help s prevent strokes caused by hypertension. Improves exercise endurance 3 It has been used to improve or maintain endurance performance of athletes during training or competition by increasing biological factors associated with oxygen uptake. In-vivo experiments have shown that oral treatment with R. rosea extracts in rats can significantly prolong the duration of long-distance swimming and activate the synthesis or re-synthesis of ATP in mitochondria, which acts as the main energy source for most cellular functions and is essential for locomotion and respiration. 2 Supplementation can reduce serum BUN, ALT and AST levels.[12] Anti-inflammatory Anti-stress [6] Anti-depression [7] . Rhodiola rosea L. roots have potent anti-depressant activity by inhibiting MAO A and may also find application in the control of senile dementia by their inhibition of MAO B.[13] Alleviates anxiety [8] Catechol-O-methyl-transferase (COMT) inhibiting effect, potentially enhancing the activity of presynaptic released dopamine.[5] Prevents damaging effect of microwave radiation. Hepatoprotective effect [9] Decreased lipid peroxidation. Gui zhi and hong jing tian extracts may be effective for correcting hyperglycemia and preventing diabetic complications.[11] Suppresses the biological enzymes of HCV NS3, thereby preventing the replication of hepatitis C virus. Promotes the release of norepinephrine from rat penile corpus cavernosum smooth muscle cell and artery endothelium cell, which was correlated with its effect of resisting senility. [ 14] The adaptogenic, cardiopulmonary protective, and central nervous system activities of Rhodiola rosea have been attributed primarily to its ability to influence levels and activity of monoamines and opioid peptides such as beta-endorphins. [15] Prolongs the life span of fruit flies by about 24% - 25% that were fed with the extract of the herb.
