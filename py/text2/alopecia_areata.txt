Auto-immune dysfunction type (5%): usually occurs after the age of 40. Hypertension type (4%): occurs in young people and adults. Hypertension occurs in one or both parents. Baldness occurs rapidly. Total baldness is about 39%. Normal type (83%): those do not fit in I, II, II type. Occurs mainly in late childhood or in young people. The duration of the disease is usually within 3 years. The patches of baldness can recover in 6 months. 6% of cases develop total baldness. Treatment A lopecia areata after child birth fu ling 10 g. Boil with water and use as tea, 2 times a day. External application: use tincture of han lian cao and bu gu zhi Treated 8 patients. All healed within 2 months.
