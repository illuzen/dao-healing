The water decoction of the herb possesses the destroying effect on the stable splitting of the Hela cells of c ervi c uterine in vitro. Using cell breathing method experiment, it showed that it can inhibit the leukemia cells above 75%. Alcohol extract of the herb possesses the inhibiting effect on transplanted tumor S-180 on mice, Ehrlich ascites tumor , brain tumor-22. Intravenous injection of the extract on animals showed hypotensive effect but oral application did not produce the same effect. The yellow needle like crystals of alcohol extract showed that it possesses diuretic effect on animals in experiments. The water decoction of the herb possesses the inhibiting effect on Staphylococcus aureus , Shigella dysenteriae , Salmonella typhi , and Escherichia coli .
