Complementary and Alternative Healing University 中藥詞典 Chinese Herb Dictionary Search this site 在本網站找資料 bi bo 蓽撥 (in English and Chinese) Translated b y: Joe Hing K wok Chu Present Day Applications: 現代應用： For toothache: bi po 6 g, gao liang jiang 3 g, chuan jiao 25 g, sheng chuan wu 0.5 g, sheng cao wu 0.5 g, yang jin hua 0.2 g. Add 100 cc of 75% alcohol. Soak for a week and add zhang nao (camphor) 2 g. Cover tightly. Ready for use. To apply: soak up some liguid with cotton ball. Bite between teeth. It will stop pain in one minute. 牙痛： 蓽撥 6 g, 高良薑 3 g, 川椒 25 g, 生川烏 0.5 g. 生草烏 0.5 g, 洋金花0.2 g. 加 75% 酒精100 c.c. 。浸一週後加樟脑2 g。盖密。用時用棉花做成一小粒。醮一些液体。放入牙齒間咬住。一分鐘即可止痛。 Pharmaceutical name: 英文药名： Fructus Piperis Longi Biological name: 學名﹕ Piper Longum ( Thumb ), Linn. Pron. in Japanese: 日語發音： hihatsu Pron. in Korean: 韓語發音： p'ihal Pron. in Cantonese : 粤語發音： bat1 but6 Other Names: 別名﹕ See Names used different parts of the world. Common Name: 常用英文名﹕ long pepper, Jaborandi pepper, Bengal pepper Distribution: 分佈﹕ Yunnan, Guandong. 雲 南、廣東等地。 Properties (characteristics): 性味: acrid, warm, hot 辛 ﹐溫﹐辣。 Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕ stomach, large intestine 胃 ﹐大腸。 Actions & Indications: 主治﹕ for cold and painful stomach, lack of appetite, vomiting, migraine, cold type of diarrhea, asthma. External usage: for toothache. 用 于胃腹冷痛，食欲不振，腎寒，偏頭痛﹐嘔吐，寒瀉，哮喘。外治牙疼。 Medical function: 藥理作用﹕ inhibit secretion of digestive juice, lowers total stomach acid lower blood lipids: lowers LDL and VLDL and TC, prevent hardening of arteries. calming effect on CNS antibiotic effect anti HBV 抑制消化液分泌，降低總胃酸 降低血脂：降低低密度脂蛋白和極低密度脂蛋白和總脂蛋白，防止血管硬化 對中樞神經系統的鎮靜效果 抗生素後效應 抗乙肝病毒 Samples of formulae: 處方舉例﹕ chronic cold type of diarrhea: bi po 9 g, water 500 cc. Simmer down to 250 cc. Drink while warm.[a] (do not use milk if patient is lactose intolerant). 長期寒性腹瀉： 蓽 撥 9 g, 加水500 c.c. 慢火煎至250c.c. 温服。如病人不能消化 乳糖 ，不可喝牛奶。 Chemical ingredients:[1] 化學成份: [1] The fruit contains: 蓽撥果實含： piperine 胡椒鹼 palmitic acid 棕櫚酸 tetrahydropiperic acid 四氫胡椒酸 l-undecylenyl-3，4-methylene-dioxybenzene 十一碳-1-烯-3，4-甲撐二氧苯 piperidine 哌啶 evaporating oil 揮發油 N -isobutyldeca-trans-2-trans-4- dienamide N-異丁基癸二烯-反2-反4-酰胺 sesamin 芝麻素 pipernonaline 蓽撥壬二烯哌啶 piperundecalidine 蓽撥撥十一碳三烯哌啶 piperlonguminine 蓽撥明寧鹼 dihydropiperlonguminine 二氫蓽撥明寧鹼 pipercide 胡椒酰胺 guineensine 幾內亞胡椒酰胺 N-isobutyloc-tadeca-2E，4E-dienamide N-異丁基十八碳-2，4-二烯酰胺 N-isobutyleicosa-2E，4E-dienamide N-異丁基二十碳-2，4-二烯酰胺 N-isobutyleicosa-2E，4E，8(Z)-trienamide N-異丁基二十碳-2，4，8-三烯酰胺 dehydropipernonaline 蓽撥壬三烯哌啶 stem contains: 莖含 piplartine,piperlongumine 蓽撥明鹼 Seed contains: 種子中含: sylvatine 長柄胡椒鹼 diaeudesmin 雙異桉脂素 Dosage: 用量: 1.5 to 4.5 g.
