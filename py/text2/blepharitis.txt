Blepharitis is a stubborn disease that is difficult to heal. The following therapy methods can help relieve the discomfort. External washing and steaming method: da huang 30 g, jin yin hua 30 g, gan cao 15 g, ju hua 15 g, bo he 30 g (packed separately, and put in after water is boiling) Wash all herbs. Boil with water except bo he. After water is boiling drop in bo he. Turn off stove. Put the pot on the table and use a towel to form a tent to cover the head and the pot. Steam the affected eye(s) with the steam from the herbs, two or 3 times a day, for about 10 minutes each time. The herb can be used a few time. Add new bo he each time. If the above method does not not solve the problem, use the following method: huang lian 30 g. Soak in water for one hour. Heat high heat until boiling; then simmer till small amount. Use a clean brush to apply to the affected eye(s) lid. Do not let the solution contact the eyes if the equipments are not sterilized.
