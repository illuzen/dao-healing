Complementary and Alternative Healing University 中藥詞典 Chinese Herb Dictionary Search this site 在本網站找資料 Chronic Morning Diarrhea Edited by: Joe Hing Kwok Chu Name of Formula: jiang huo sheng shi tang (Nei Wai Shang Bian Huo Lun) Diagnostics: For primarily pixu and secondarily shenyangxu Prescription: j iang huo , du huo 6 grams each; gao ben , fang feng , zhi gan cao , chuan xiong 3 grams each; man jing zi 2 grams. Boil herbs with water. Take as tea. Adjustment to the formula: If the diagnosis is shenyangxu , the treatment should be using warming and astringent formula. I f the diagnosis is primarily pixu and secondarily shenyangxu , then add the following to the formula: bai zhi , sheng ma , ge gen , cang zhu , bai zhu , bai shao . Adjust formula according to diagnosis. Or the following formula can also be selected: bu gu zhi 15 g, da zao 15 g, rou dou kou 5 g, sheng jiang 10 g. Add 800 cc of water. Simmer till 300 cc. Make into 2 servings. Take in the morning and evening.
