Make into powder. Take before meal with drinking water. Note: Zhong Hua Ben Cao : Large dosage is toxic. In mild cases, the clinical symptoms include: In mild toxicity: lack of strength, lack of energy, lack of appetite, nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, diarrhea or constipation, followed by dizziness, headache, sleepiness or restlessness and anxiety, tachycardia or bradycardia, low fever, sweating, flush cheeks with slightly jaundice around the nose and mouth, swollen liver.
