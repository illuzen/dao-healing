Other Names: jiang jie shi 姜結石, jiang jie shi 僵結石, jiang shi 姜石, wu li shi 蕪礪石, jiang shi 礓礫, wu li 蕪礪 Uses: leukemia, tumors. In some southern parts of China, it is use for brewing tea and dropping into water wells to lower the nitrites content of the water . Characteristics: salty, cold, nontoxic. Medical functions: anti-fungal, antibiotic, anti tumors, expels phlegm , lowers the nitrites content of water . Chemical ingredients: Calcium carbonate, and other trace minerals: zinc, selenium, chromium, iron, tungsten, manganese, germanium, tin, sodium, etc. 料薑石 別名： 姜 結石, 僵結石, 姜石, 蕪礪石, 礓礫, 蕪礪 分佈： 料姜石主產於甘肅，陝西一帶 性味： 鹹，寒，無毒 藥理 ﹕ 有 止血消 炎，利痰 ， 抗 細菌，抗真菌，破壞癌細胞的作用。 可 用 來降低水中亞硝酸鹽的含量。 用途﹕ 癌 症，白血症。 中國南方有些地區將了薑石放入水井中或用來泡茶， 可能 有 防癌作用。 化學成份﹕ 碳 酸鈣，與其他微量元素， 如鋅，硒，鉻, 鐵，鎢，錳 ，鍺, 錫，鈉 等成份。 A manuscript... writing, translating and proofreading in progress Click here for inquiry Copyright Notice 按此看關於版權問題 List of Health Problems Chinese Herb Dictionary Toxicity of Some Herbs Side Effect of Some Herbs Herbs that can be toxic to kidneys Traditional Chinese Medicine TCM Diagnosis Samples of Formulae Acupuncture copy right 版權所有 Qigong (chi kung) Prescribed Drug Dictionary Email our web master for your suggestion (not for inquiry) Biochemistry Dictionary General Online Library Qigong classes Fake Email Warning Research Librarian Search Qigong classes Home Weather Report Disclaimer Last update: Nov 12, 2008; 10:31 p .m.
