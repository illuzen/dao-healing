When for no obvious reasons, normal periods stop in women before 40 years old, it is considered as premature ovarian failure. The symptoms can include: Amenorrhea Irregular periods Hot flashes Night sweats Difficulty concentrating or focusing on tasks Irritability Painful sex, lack of lubrication Atrophy of Labia Minora Decrease in the amount of creases in the wall of the vagina Decrease in the elasticity of the wall of the vagina Red or pale labia Low libido Vaginal atrophy or dryness Hypo-estrogenism Low level of serum estradiol (E 2 ) High level of serum gonadotropin (e.g. LH and FSH) Low cortisol Low aldosterone Osteoporosis （decreased bone density） T hyroid dysfunction (i.e., Hashimotos thyroiditis) Hair coarseness Sleep disorder Increased appetite / weight gain Hair Loss suicidal thought / depression Decreased fertility (5 percent and 10 percent of women with POF become pregnant without treatment [1] [1] http://www.nichd.nih.gov/publications/pubs/pof/ Report #1 Name of Formula: Gui ShenTang ( Source of formula: jin yue quan shu） with added ingredients. Composition: tu si zi 20 g, gou qi zi 15 g, du zhong 12 g, shan zhu yu 12 g, dang gui 12 g, shu di huang 15 g, shan yao 30 g, fu ling 30 g, gui Jia 15 g (to be boiled longer than the rest of the herb), e jiao 10 g One combination of these herbs was decocted in water per day and made into 2 servings. Adjustments of Formula: If hot flashes and sweating were severe, we added : shu di huang 20 g, xuan shen 15 g, dan pi 12 g If insomnia or profuse dreams were severe, we added: zhen zhu mu 15 g* If low back and knee soreness and limpness were severe, we added: xu duan 20 g *Editor's Note: osteoporosis or decreased bone density: zhen zhu mu can be added.
