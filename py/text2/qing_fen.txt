Usually use with other water-removing herbs in difficulty in urination and defecation. 外用攻毒殺虫：用於疥，癬，梅毒。 內服利水通便：用於水腫鼓脹，二便不利者，配逐水藥。 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ mercury chloride (Hg2Cl2 or HgCl) 氯 化亞汞或氯化汞 Dosage: 用量﹕ 0.1 to 0.2 g taken in pills or powder form. Use appropriate amount for external application. 内服 0.1～0.2g，入丸散。外用適量。 Samples of formulae: 處方舉例﹕ Modern Research: 現代研究﹕ Cautions: 禁忌﹕ do not overdose and not for long term use. After taking this herb, rinse the mouth immediately to prevent injury to the mouth.
