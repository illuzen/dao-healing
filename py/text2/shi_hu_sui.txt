A.Br.et Aschers Pron. in Japanese: 日語發音﹕ Pron. in Korean: 韓語發音﹕ Pron. in Cantonese: 粵音﹕ Other Names: 別名﹕ tian hu sui 天胡荽，ye yuan sui 野圆荽，e bu shi cao 鹅不食草，jichang cao 雞腸草。 Common Name: 英文名﹕ Distribution: 產地﹕ Mainly produced in Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Hubei, Guangdong. Also produced in Anhui, Jiangxi, Fujian, Henan, Guangxi and other provinces. 主產於浙江，江蘇，湖北，廣東。安徽，江西，福建，河南，廣西等地亦產。 Properties (characteristics)﹕ 性味﹕ acrid, warm. 辛，溫。 Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕ lung, liver. 肺，肝。 Medical functions: 藥理﹕ Actions & Indications: 主治﹕ For cold or flu with headache, cough with abundance of phlegm, suffy nose, chronic sinus infammation, whooping cough, chronic bronchitis, conjunctivitis, malaria. 用於風寒頭痛，咳嗽痰多，鼻塞不通，鼻淵流涕，百日咳，慢性支氣管炎，結膜炎，瘧疾。 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ Sitosterols: 固醇類： Taraxerol 蒲公英賽醇 Taraxasterol 蒲公英甾醇 Taraxasteryl palmitate 蒲公英甾醇棕櫚酸酯 Campesterol 菜油甾醇 Stigmasterol 豆甾醇 β -sitosterol β - 谷甾醇 Arnidiol 山金車烯二醇 Evaporating oil 1%: 揮發油 0.1% : Palmitic acid)56% 棕櫚酸 56% α - ，β -Pinene α - β - 菠烯 Other: 其他 此外，還有: Oganic acid 有機酸 Tannin 鞣質 Resin 樹脂 Coumarin 香豆素 Lactone 內酯 Dosage: 用量﹕ For decoction: 6 to 9 g.
