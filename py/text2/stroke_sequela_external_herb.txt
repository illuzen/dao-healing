Sequela after Strokes with Swollen Limbs- External Formula translated by Joe Hing Kwok Chu 朱興國編譯 按此看中文 Sequela after strokes with swollen limbs. Prescription: tou gu cao 30 g , chuan shan jia 30 g ， ji xing zi 15 g , jiang huang 15 g , san leng 15 g , e zhu 15 g , han fang ji 15 g , wei ling xian 15 g. Add 1,500 cc of water and simmer down to 500 cc. Wash and steam the affected areas, twice a day, 30 minutes each time. Seven day period is one therapy period. Continue for 2 to 3 periods. Rest 2 to 3 days between periods. Note: chuan shan jia is a protected animal and is not legal in commerce in most countries. See ba wei shun qi wan .
