Pron. in Japanese: 日語發音﹕ sayo Pron. in Korean: 韓語發音﹕ seoyang Pron. in Cantonese : 粵語發音： so 2 y e ong 4 Other Names: 別名﹕ di mao qiu 地毛球、 xiao tie bang 鏽鐵棒、 suo yan zi 鎖嚴子。 Common Name: 英文名﹕ Songaria Cynomorium Herb. Distribution: 產地﹕ Xinjiang, Gansu, Qinghai, Inner Mongolia, Ningxia. Mainly in Gansu, Inner Mongolia, Ningxia. Also being produced in Ningxia, and Qinghai. 分佈於新疆、甘肅、青海、內蒙古、寧夏等地。主產甘肅、新疆、內蒙古。寧夏、青海等地亦產。 Properties characteristics)﹕ 性味﹕ sweet, warm . 甘、溫。 Channels meridians) entered: 歸經﹕ l spleen, kidney, large intestine. 入脾，腎經，大腸。 Medical functions: 藥理﹕ 1. 對免疫及性功能的影響：使大鼠血漿睪酮含量顯著增高。鎖陽還可對抗長期緊張等因素引起的小鼠性行為減少。 2.對腸功能的影響：鎖陽對臨床老年便秘的治療，將是一個較為理想的藥物。 3．其它作用：抗癌。鎖陽在一定程度上抗激素對腎上腺皮質的外源性抑制作用。 Actions & Indications: 主治﹕ impotence, constipation due to anemia, lack of strength in loin and knee areas. 陽萎，血枯便秘，腰膝軟弱。 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ Whole herb contains: 全草含: cynoterpene 鎖陽萜 acetylursolic acid 已酰熊果酸 ursolic acid 熊果酸 fat contain: 脂肪油中含: alkanes mixture (0.07%) 鏈烷烴混合物（0.07%） glyceride (0.79%) 甘油酯（0.79%） Fatty acid mainly consists of: 脂肪酸組成主要為 palmitic acid 櫚酸 olic acid 油酸 inoleic acid 油酸 sterol (0.01%) 甾醇（0.01%）: β-sitosterol β-谷甾醇 sterol palmitate 膽固醇棕櫚酸酯 胡蘿卜甾醇daucosterol also contains tannin (about 7%) 還含鞣質（約7%） campesterol 菜油甾醇 β-sitosterol palmitate β-谷甾醇棕櫚酸酯 aspartic acid 天冬氨酸 proline 脯氨酸 serine 絲氨酸 alanine 丙氨酸 Dosage: 用量﹕ 10 g to 18 g 4 ~ 15 克 Samples of formulae:[1] 處方舉例﹕[1] For sh enxu (kidney deficient) with spermatorrhea the impotence: Prescription: suo yang, long gu, ruo cong rong , sang piao xiao, fu ling, equal amount of each and make into powder. Mix with cooked honey to make into pills. Each serving is 9 grams, for morning and evening. ("Ningxia herbal Manual") For treatment of impotence, premature ejaculation: Prescription: suo yang 15 g, dang shen, shan yao 12 g each, fu peng zi 9 g. Boil with water and server as tea.. ("Shaanxi-Gansu-Ningxia green herbal medicine Selection") For treatment of senior with qixu (qi deficient ) and yinxu (yin deficient) , dry stool: Prescription: suo yang, sang shen 15 g each. Make into thick decoction and add 30 g of white honey. Make into 2 servings. ("Ningxia herbal Manual") For treatment of urinary tract infection with hematuria: Prescription: suo yang, ren dong teng , 15 g each, mao gen 30 g. Boil with water and take as tea. ("Ningxia herbal Manual") For treatment of weak digestion: Prescription: suo yang 15 g. Boil with water and take as tea. 治 腎虛 遺精，陽萎: 處方：鎖陽、 龍骨 、 肉蓯蓉 、 桑螵蛸 、 茯苓 各等分。共研末，煉蜜為丸，每服三錢，早晚各一次。 (《寧夏中草藥手冊》) 治陽萎，早泄: 處方：鎖陽五錢， 黨 參 ， 山藥 各四錢， 覆盆子 三錢。水煎服。 (《陝甘寧青中草藥選》) 治老年 氣虛 陰虛 ，大便燥結: 處方：鎖陽、 桑椹 各五錢。水煎取濃汁加白蜂蜜一兩，分兩次服。 (《寧夏中草藥手冊》) 治泌尿系感染尿血: 處方：鎖陽、 忍冬藤 各五錢， 茅根 一兩。水煎服。 (《寧夏中草藥手冊》) 治消化不良： 處方：鎖陽五錢。水煎服。 Modern Research: 現代研究﹕ Toxicity & Cautions: 毒素與禁忌﹕ not to use in cases of shenyinxu (kidney yin deficient) with heat, or diarrhea due to pixu (spleen deficient) or constipation with excess heat.
