Its qi enhancing effect is not as strong as ren shen (ginseng). It better suits those with weak function and yinxu (yin deficient) and those who cough due to lung qi deficient, yinxu (yin deficient) ; for chronic and acute hepatitis.. It is commonly used as substitute for ren shen and for huang qi in cases of hypertension. Compared to xi yang shen (American ginseng), it is a better choice for correcting yinxu condition. 益氣滋陰生津。但補氣之力遠不及 人 參 。 較重於 滋 陰 ﹐生津止渴﹐調肺潤 燥 。 宜 于 陰 虛 體弱 者﹔ 肺 氣不足 陰 虛 咳嗽。 急慢性肝 炎。處 方時有血壓偏高情況者﹐常用太子參代 替 人 參 、 黃耆 。 比起 西洋參 ﹐太子參養陰之力較強。 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ 太子参皂 甙A (taizishen saponin A)﹐ 棕櫚酸 ( palmitic acid )﹐ 亞油酸 ( linoleic acid ) , β－谷 甾醇 ( β -sitosterol) ， 糖 (sugar) 磷脂 (phosphatides), 氨基酸 (amino acid), 含有 16 種氨基酸，其中人體必需的 八種。 佔 氨基酸總量的 40%。 It contains 16 amino acid, among them, eight kinds of the amino acid, constituting 40% of the total amino acid, are required by the human body. 揮發油 (essential oil) 。 微量元素 ﹕ 鐵﹐銅 ﹐錳﹐鋅﹐硒﹐鈷﹐鉬 等。 Trace elements of : iron, copper, manganese, zinc, selenium, cobalt, molybdenum etc. Dosage: 用量﹕ 10 ~ 30 g Samples of formulae: 處方舉例﹕ Samples of formulae: Qi deficient and blood deficient after illness: tai zi shen 15 g, huang qi 12 g, wu wei zi 3 g, chao bai bian dou 9 g, da zao 4 pieces. Boil with water. Use as tea Neurasthenia: tai zi she 15 g, dang gu i, suan zao ren, yuan zhi, shi gan cao 9 g each. Boil with water. Use as tea. Pixu, weak digestion, loose stool, and lack of appetite: tai zi xhen 12 g, bai zhu 9 g, fu ling 9 g, chen pi 6 g, gan cao 6 g. Boil with water. Use as tea. 處方舉例﹕ 病後血血 虧虚：太子參15g， 黃 耆 12g， 五味子 3g，炒 白 扁 豆 9g， 大 棗 4枚 。 水 煎服。 神經衰弱：太子參 15g， 當 歸 、酸棗仁、遠志、炙 甘 草 各9g。水煎服。 脾虚便溏、食欲不振：太子參 12g， 白 朮 、 茯 苓 各9g， 陳皮 、 甘 草 各6g，水煎服。 以上處方来源：上海熱線 Warning: 禁忌﹕ Overdosing may cause undesirable effect. 過量服用可能有不良反應。 Note: Two of the Chinese classics, Ben Cao Cong Xin 《本草從新》 and Ben Cao Gang Mu Shi Yi 《本草綱目拾遺 》 mentioned that tai zi shen was "ren shen of the small sizes" The tai zi shen being used today and being discussed in this article is the roots of a completely different plant, not the small sizes ren shen (Panx ginseng) as mentioned in those classics, but is Pseudostellaria heterophylla (miq) Pax ex Pax et Hoffm. . 《本草從新》和《本草 綱目拾遺》所載的太子參，均為五加科人參之小者。 并非本品。 Sponsors' Ads by Google 以下為谷歌所提供贊助者之廣告 A manuscript... writing, translating and proofreading in progress Click here for inquiry Copyright Notice 按此看關於版權問題 List of Health Problems Chinese Herb Dictionary Toxicity of Some Herbs Side Effect of Some Herbs Herbs that can be toxic to kidneys Traditional Chinese Medicine TCM Diagnosis Samples of Formulae Acupuncture copy right 版權所有 Qigong (chi kung) Prescribed Drug Dictionary Email our web master for your suggestion (not for inquiry) Biochemistry Dictionary General Online Library Qigong classes Fake Email Warning Research Librarian Search Qigong classes Home Weather Report Disclaimer Last update: July 15, 2009 10:30 p .m.
