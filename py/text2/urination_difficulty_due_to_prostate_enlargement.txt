Traditionally, surgery is the main solution but more and more patients are selecting non surgery treatments. One of the main causes is that testosterone turns into excess dihydrotestosterone which stimulate the cells of the prostate glands and causes the cells to multiply which causes the enlargement of the prostate glands. Urinary tract infection or prostate inflammation, or blood circulation stasis in the pelvic area also can make the symptoms worse. The medication available for prostate enlargement mainly falls under the following categories: 1. adrenergic blocker 2. reductase inhibitors 3. leuteinization stimulating hormone 4. anti cholestrol drugs 5. anti testosterone drugs 6. herbal remedies Certain h erbal remedies can possess the functions of the above medications.
