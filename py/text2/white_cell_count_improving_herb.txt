Composition: ren shen 6 g, bai zhu 6 g, fu ling 6 g, huang qi 6 g, dang gui 9 g, suan zao ren 6 g, long yan rou 12 g, yuan zhi 6 g , zhi gan cao 6 g, da zao 12 g, mu xiang 6 g, sheng jiang 12 g, shu di huang 18 g, shan zhu yu 12 g, dan pi 9 g, ze xie 9 g, shan yao 12 g.
