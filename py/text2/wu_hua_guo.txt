Last update: Oct 3 1, 2010, 11:1 7 p .m. LAH wu hua guo 無花果 by: Joe Hing kwok Chu Pharmaceutical name: 英文藥名﹕ Fructus fici, Folium fici. Botanical name: 拉丁生物 學名﹕ Ficus carica L. Pron. in Japanese: 日語發音﹕ ichijiku , chitsutomo Pron. in Korean: 韓語發音﹕ muhwagwa Pron. in Cantonese :: 粵音﹕ mou4 faa1 gwo2 Other Names: 別名﹕ Common Name: 英文名﹕ fig Distribution: 產地﹕ Originated from south western part of Asia. Now they are also grown in most part of China 原産於亞洲西南地區。現在中國各地均有生産。 Properties (characteristics)﹕ 性味﹕ sweet, cool. 甘，凉。 Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕ lung, spleen, stomach. 肺 ，脾，胃。 Medical functions: 藥理﹕ See: http://scialert.net/fulltext/?doi=rjmp.2011.246.253&org=10 Actions & Indications: 主治﹕ Fruit: being used in heat in lung with coarse voice, dry coughs, constipation, weak digestion, hemorrhoids, prolapse of rectum. Leaves: being used in diabetes, tumors. 果用於：肺熱聲嘶，乾咳，便秘，消化不良，痔瘡，脫肛。 葉用於：糖尿病，癌腫。 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ citric acid 檸檬酸 fumaric acid 延胡索酸 succinic acid 琥珀酸 malonic acid 丙二酸 (縮蘋果酸) proline 脯氨酸 oxalic acid 草酸 malic acid 蘋果酸 shikimic acid 莽草酸 quinic acid 奎尼酸 alkaloids 生物鹼 glucosides 甙類 sugar 糖類 ficin 無花果朊酶 fiber 纖維 magnesium,copper, manganese, iron, calcium, sodium, zinc, selenijm. 鎂，酮，錳，鐵，鈣，鈉，鋅，硒。 vitamins A,B,C, K. folic acid 维生素 A, B, C, K, 葉酸。 Leaves contain: 葉含： psoralens 補骨脂素 bergapten 佛手柑内酯 selenium 硒 Daly Dosage: 每日用量﹕ Samples of formulae: 處方舉例﹕ For pylorus cancer: use wu hua guo (figs) to make into decoction. Take orally 3 times per day. 無花果不拘量，水煎內服，每日三次，可治胃幽門癌。《常氏方》 Modern Research: 現代研究﹕ liver protective effect: http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2792510/ 無花果之陰乾樹葉石油醚提取物對大鼠有保肝作用。請按 此 聯線。 （英文報導）。 Toxicity & Cautions: 毒素與禁忌﹕ When taken leaf decoction, avoid direct sunlight. Not to use in cases of pixu (spleen deficient) with loose stool. Long term taking this fruit may suppress appetite. 脾虛 便溏者不宜吃無花果。服用葉煎剤期間，不宜直接晒太陽以免晒黑皮膚。 長期食用無花果可能導致食慾减退。 Sponsors' Ads by Google 以下為谷歌所提供贊助者之廣告 A manuscript... writing, translating and proofreading in progress Problems with website?
