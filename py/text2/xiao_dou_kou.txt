The evaporating oil is about 2 to 10%, mainly consists of d-borneol, d-camphor, cineole, cymene etc. 揮發油 ， 少量皂甙、色素和澱粉等。揮發油含量為2∼10％ ；揮發油的主要成分為d-龍腦(d-Borneol)、d-樟腦(d-Camphor)及桉油素傘花烴。 Daly Dosage: 每日用量﹕ Samples of formulae: 處方舉例﹕ Modern Research: 現代研究﹕ Toxicity & Cautions: 毒素與禁忌﹕ Do not use in cases of blood deficient or qixu (qi deficiency) and fire is high in lung and stomach with no cold dampness (as in chronic yeast infection). In India, traditionally cardamom are dried using wood to provide the heat. The smoke from the fire can produce antifungal effect but long term consumption of smoked product can be carcinogenic . 肺胃火盛及 氣 虚 或 血 虛 ,無寒濕實邪 （如長期性念珠球菌感染）者禁 用。 傳统上，在印度， 小豆蔻是用木材焼火烘乾的。木材焼出之烟雖有防霉作用但長期服食可能會致癌。 [1] J Med Food. 2012 Jun;15(6):576-80. doi: 10.1089/jmf.2011.0266. Epub 2012 Mar 9. [2] Indian J Biochem Biophys. 2009 Dec;46(6):503-6. [3] J Med Food. 2010 Apr;13(2):371-81. doi: 10.1089/jmf.2009.1131.
