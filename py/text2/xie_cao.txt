Pharmaceutical name: 英文藥名﹕ Rhizome Valerianae, Radix Valerianae Latin name: 拉丁學名﹕ Valeriana officinalis Linn. Valeriana amurensi s P. Smirn （ 黑水纈草） Pron. in Japanese: 日語發音﹕ kekusa Pron. in Korean: 韓語發音﹕ seol-ryeong-jwi-o-jum-pul Pron. in Cantonese: 粵音﹕ kit3 cou2 Other Names: 別名 ﹕ ba di ma 拔地麻、xiao jiu jia in Shaanxi) 小救 駕[陜西]、lu zi cao 鹿子草、chou cao 臭草 Common Name: 英文名﹕ valerian, all-heal, garden heliotrope Distribution: 產地﹕ Shaanxi, Gansu, Qinghai, Xinjiang, Sichuan, Hebei, Henan, Shandong, Shanxi, Taiwan, Hubei provinces of China, also in western Asia and Europe. 分佈陝西、甘肅、青海、新疆、四川、河北、河南、山 東、山西、台灣、湖北等地。亞洲西部和歐洲也有。 Properties characteristics)﹕ 性味﹕ acrid, sweet, warm 辛、甘，溫。 Channels meridians) entered: 歸經﹕ heart, pericardium, spleen. 心，心包， 脾。 Medical functions: 藥理﹕ Medical functions: 1. Effect on CNS: xie cao (valerian) possesses a sedative effect and is frequently used as a sedative. It can enhance the inhibition of the cortex of the brain, lower the excitement re-action, and eliminate smooth muscle spasm. It is also being used to prevent cardio-vascular diseases and in hyperthyroidism. Mixed with bromine agent, it counter-acts drug-induced excitement. 2. Effect on respiration and circulation: Certain active ingredient in xie cao (valerian) can lower the blood pressure of certain animals like cats, rabbits and mice. That is because xie cao can mimic the para-sympathetic nervous system, block the reflect of carotid sinus and inhibit the CNS. 3. Effect of antibiotics: The total alkaloid from xie cao (valerian) possesses an antibiotic effect, especially toward Gram positive bacteria . 4. Effect of GABA: Animal tests showed that xie cao (valerian) can promote the release of γ-aminobutyric acid ( GABA) and create the effect of sedation and can help the γ-aminobutyric acid in the cortex of the brain to promote sleep. 藥理﹕ ( 一) 對神經系統的作用 缬草有鎮靜作用，能加強大腦皮質的抑制作用，減低反射興奮性，解除平滑肌痙攣，可與溴劑並用於各種藥物興奮狀態，可防治心血管神經 官能症，甲狀腺機能亢進症等。 ( 二) 對循環呼吸的作用 缬草中的某些成分對動物 貓、兔、小鼠等)有降低血壓作用，此作用與其擬副交感神經，阻斷頸動脈竇反射及抑制中樞系統有關。 ( 三) 抗菌作用 從缬草裡提出的總生物鹹有抗菌作用，特別是對革蘭氏陽性的細菌效力較好。 (四） 動物實驗證明 缬 草能促進大腦皮質釋放 γ-氨基丁酸 ( GABA)， 發揮鎮靜 作用，並且確能促進大腦皮質裡的 γ-氨 基 丁酸 （ GABA)發 揮催眠效果。 Actions & Indications: 主治﹕ The rhizoma and roots are being used as medicine. It is being used in sedating, pain relieving, neurasthenia, insomnia, epilepsy, flatulence and pain in the abdomen, pain in loin and legs, injuries from impact. Europeans have been using it for treating anxiety. 根 狀茎及根入药。有 安神，理氣，止痛之作用。用于神經衰弱，失眠，癲癇，胃腹脹痛，腰腿痛，跌打損傷。 歐洲人 常用纈草來治療焦慮症 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ Valerianic acid 缬草酸 ( 戊 酸 ) Valerine 缬草鹹 Valeranone 缬草酮 Valerenone 缬草萜烯醇 Valerianine 缬草寧鹹 Valepotriae 缬草三酯 Valerosidatum 缬草環臭蟻醛酯弋 Bornol 龍腦 Camphene 茨烯 Fenehere 葑烯 Pinen 菠烯 Eremophilene 雅檻藍樹油烯 terpineol 松油醇 Mireene 日桂烯 Limonene 檸檬烯 phellandrene 水芹烯 Caryophyllene 石竹烯 Alloaromadendrene 別香燈烯 Dosage: 用量﹕ 3 to 6 g (dried herb).
