Pharmaceutical name: 英文藥名﹕ Fructus Uvae Ursi , Folium Uvae Ursi Botanical name: 學名﹕ Arctostaphylos uva-ursi (L.) Spreng Pron. in Japanese: 日語發音﹕ kuma kokemomo （熊苔桃） Pron. in Korean: 韓語發音﹕ Pron. in Cantonese: 粵音﹕ hung 4 mui4 Other Names: 別名﹕ Common Name: 英文名﹕ bearberry, kinnikinnick, pinemat manzanita, carillo, mealberry, mountain box, mountain cranberry, hog cranberry, sandberry, sagackhomi, bear's berry, arberry. Distribution: 產地﹕ northern latitudes and high mountains of Europe, Asia and America. 歐洲，亞洲，與美洲，北部與高山 地區。 Properties (characteristics)﹕ 性味﹕ bitter 味苦 Channels (meridians) entered: 歸經﹕ Medical functions: 藥理﹕ Arbutin exercises an antiseptic effect on the urinary mucous membrane, inhibits production of melanin, heals cuts and anti-inflammatory. 抑制黑色素的生成， 利 尿, 防腐 , 潤膚 ，愈創，殺菌和協同抗炎。 。 Actions & Indications: 主治﹕ inflammatory infection diseases of the urinary tract, urethritis, cystisis, kidney stones, hemorrhoids. As diurectics. For external use: as an astringent wash for cuts and scrapes. 用於腎及膀胱感染, 腎結石, 糖尿病及痔瘡。 當作利尿劑。 外用﹕割傷﹐劃傷。煎湯洗患處。 Chemical ingredients: 化學成份﹕ arbutin (crystallizable glucoside) 熊果苷 hydroquinone glycoside, 苯二酚 甙 methylarbutin, 甲基熊果酚甙 ericolin (an ill-defined glucoside), 石南素 ursone (a crystalline substance of resinous character), 烏索酸,熊果酸 gallic acid, 没食子酸 ellagic acid, (a yellow colouring principle resembling quercetin)s 聯二沒食子酸內酯, 逆沒食子酸, 並沒食子酸, 胡頹子酸, 鞣花酸, myricetin. 楊 梅黄酮 tannin (6 to 7 per cent). 單寧 （6～7%） ash about 3 % . 灰 份大约3% Dosage: 用量﹕ 3 g in decoction. 煎湯用3克。 Samples of formulae: 處方舉例﹕ Modern Research: 現代研究﹕ The water extract of the leaf increases the inhibitory effect of dexamethasone on the allergic and inflammatory activities.
