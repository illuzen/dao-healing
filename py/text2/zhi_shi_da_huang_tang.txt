C omposition: qiang huo , 6 g. dang gui 4 g. zhi shi 2 g. da huang 12 g (put in and cook another 8 minutes) Boil with 600 c.c. of water and render down to 400 c.c. Drink while the stomach is empty. Stop using when the bowel movements return to normal.
